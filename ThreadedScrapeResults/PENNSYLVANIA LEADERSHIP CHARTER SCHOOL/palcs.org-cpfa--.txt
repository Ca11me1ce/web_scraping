Learn more about PALCS
Welcome to the Center for Performing and Fine Arts (CPFA)! This unique adventure in arts education is truly a cutting-edge model of public school innovation. CPFA is an audition-based supplemental program for students enrolled in the 
Pennsylvania Leadership Charter School
 (PALCS) and offers a pre-professional arts education in theatre, dance, music and fine arts to students in grades 6 through 12 with exceptional aptitude, maturity and dedication.
