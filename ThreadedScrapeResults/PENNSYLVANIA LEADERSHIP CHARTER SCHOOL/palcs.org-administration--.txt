Administration
PA Leadership Charter School is overseen by a group of talented and highly qualified staff members who lead the different facets of our school. They are dedicated to student growth, achievement and providing support to new and current families. Following the 
vision and mission
, administration staff work closely together to provide our community with an environment that is collaborative, engaging and relationship focused. 
Dr. James Hanak founded PA Leadership Charter School in 2004 and has served as the CEO since the very first day of school.
