Our Media Center Is Now Open
Come See What a Great Book and Your Child's Imagination Can Do Together!
To maintain a great Library please adhere to the Media Center procedures. 
1) 2 books may be checked out at once 
2) Both books must be returned at the student's next library visit. A $ 1.00 per day late fee will be charged for overdue books. 
3) There is a $20 lost or damaged book fee. 
Please feel free to donate any books that are no longer being used, If you hear of a library having a sale please let us know.
