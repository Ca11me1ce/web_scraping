Are You a Proud Somerset Academy Alumni?
													We would love to showcase our great Panther Alumni accomplishments on our website!  If you are Somerset Academy Alumni who has a newsworthy accomplishment, please contact us!
Alumni Directory
													Are you a graduate of Somerset Academy Charter High School or Somerset Arts Conservatory?  Then register on our online Alumni Directory! Get information regarding upcoming events and reunions.  Click here to register!
Nina Florido, Somerset Alumni:  On Her Way to Law School!
													After completing undergrad in under 3 years, Nina was awarded a scholarship and acceptance to the top law school in Florida, University of Florida Levin College of Law.  She commenced her studies Fall of 2017. Moreover, she's one of the youngest 1L students at 21.
Somerset Arts Conservatory Alumni, Koine... SYTYCD Runner-Up!
													Congratulations to Somerset Arts Conservatory and Varsity Pantherettes Alumna (Class of 2015), Koine Iwasaki, has been voted the Runner-Up on Fox's hit  show "So You Think You Can Dance."  We are incredibly proud of Koine's accomplishments!
Congrats to Alum, Ariana Hundley, New Miami Heat Dancer!
													Congratulations to Somerset Arts Conservatory Class of 2017 Alumna, Ariana Hundley, who was chosen to be on the Miami Heat Dance Team!  Ariana is our first alumni to make the team.
Congrats Somerset Arts Conservatory Alumni, Arielle DiSciascio!
													Congratulations to Somerset Arts Conservatory Class of 2016 Alumna, Arielle DiSciascio, who proceeded to the next audition phase on this season's "So You Think You Can Dance!"  You can cheer for her on Mondays at 8pm on Fox!  Click here to read more about Arielle in a Sun-Sentinel article!
