A Midsummer Night's Dream
Senior Prom
2018
$110 Non-Package or Guest
Payment/Forms Deadline:  May 4th
Senior Prom
Friday, May 25th
7:30pm- Midnight
Formal Attire
The Signature Grand
*ALL Guests must complete "Senior Prom Application" by May 4th and turn in  with payment.
*Somerset Non-Panther Package Seniors and Guests MUST complete "2018 Somerset Senior Prom Guidelines" form by May 4th and turn in with payment.
**ALL students and guests must be cleared by administration in order to attend this event**
*SENIORS will NOT be allowed to participate in the Panther Package or in any 12th Grade events if they have the following or do not meet these criteria:
Absences
 5 or more unexcused absences in any      class
Tardies 
20 or more tardies to school
Finances 
Any outstanding obligations prior to the      event
Grades 
Failing any      classes in the first semester and/or any quarter
Student Volunteer Hours 
Must have a minimum of 40 hours completed      by February 2, 2018
Misconduct 
Placed in Indoor or Outdoor suspension or      misbehavior at a school sponsored event (participation      will be based on Administration’s discretion/number of infractions)
Grade Point Average (GPA) 
Failure to maintain a 2.0 average      (unweighted)
College Readiness 
Failure to take the SAT/ACT by December      2017 and completion of senior survey, hard copy and online, by May 1, 2018 in order to receive graduation tickets. 
*NO  REFUNDS WILL BE GIVEN IF REMOVED FROM ACTIVITY*
