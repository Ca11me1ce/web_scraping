SCHOOL POLICIES & HANDBOOK          
This handbook has been written to provide both parents and students with important information about the day-to-day operation of the Roosevelt Children's Academy. 
We are hopefully you will have a better understanding of the procedures,rules, and policies of the Roosevelt Children's Academy after reviewing this booklet carefully.Please keep the handbook available during the school year. It will provide a ready reference for many questions you might have.
