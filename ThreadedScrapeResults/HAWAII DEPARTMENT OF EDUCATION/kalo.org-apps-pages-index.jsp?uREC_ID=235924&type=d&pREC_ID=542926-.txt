Summer Program
 Aloha Parents!
We are happy to announce that our summer program is back for 2017!  Please see the application below.  The application is a little longer than in the past so please be sure to fill it out completely before submitting it.  
Also, we have financial aid available as well, so please be sure to fill out that portion of the application completely. 
You will receive an email confirmation once we've received your child's application and payment. 
SUMMER 2017
Grades: K to 5th Grade 
Dates: June 5th to July 7th  
Time: Monday to Thursday, 8am to 2:45pm  
NO SESSION ON FRIDAYS  
EXCEPT: Ho'ike and Potluck Lunch will be on Friday, July 7th
Mahalo Nui and we hope to see your keiki this summer!  
-te
