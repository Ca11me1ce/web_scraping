Kanu o ka ʻĀina NCPCS
Kanu o ka ʻĀina New Century Public Charter School
Kanu o ka ‘Āina New Century Public Charter School is a Hawaiian-focused, bi-lingual public charter school serving over 300 students in grades K-12, which started up in 2000 in Waimea on Hawaiʻi Island. Our motto “Kūlia i Ka Nuʻu” signifies our efforts to provide a quality choice in education, as well as our ongoing focus on growth and excellence at all levels, a standard set by Hawaiʻi’s native forefathers.
