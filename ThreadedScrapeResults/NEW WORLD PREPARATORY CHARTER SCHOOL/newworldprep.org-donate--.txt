Other Ways to Give
There are many ways you can choose to make a donation to New World Prep. You can use our donate tab and make a donation through PayPal or you can:
Give by Check:
Please make checks payable to “New World Prep Annual Fund” and mail
to:
Office of the President
New World Prep
26 Sharpe Avenue
Staten Island, New York 10302
Corporate Matching Gifts:
Many companies will match their employees’ charitable contributions.
Please check with your employer to see if they will match your gift to
New World Prep.  You can fill out the company’s matching gift form and
send it to us with your gift.
Gifts-in-Kind:
Many non-cash donations of materials that support and serve the school are needed and welcome. These gifts may include items such as equipment, furniture, technology, design materials or classroom supplies. Contact the Office of the President at (718) 705-8990, extension 223 for more details.
