Click here for Online enrollment
You may bring necessary documents to the school if you are unable to upload them.
ALL ENROLLMENT IS DONE ON LINE. YOU ARE WELCOME TO COME TO THE SCHOOL TO USE OUR COMPUTER FOR THE ENROLLMENT PROCESS.
Enrollment checklist- 
Help speed up enrollment by bringing along these important forms: (**students will not be enrolled in school without these forms)
**Birth Certificate
A birth certificate is required for all new students who haven't been enrolled in the district before.
**Proof of Immunization--SHOT RECORDS
**Proof of Current Physical Address
Utility bills such as phone, water or electric-proof of one of these services at the home resident is required
Reminder: Students must be 5 years old on or before September 1st to enroll in Kindergarten
OAA REQUESTS: If you do not live in the Joy attendance area and wish to have your child attend Joy you will need to enroll in your home school and us this 
OAA lottery information link 
to learn more about how to make an OAA request online. 
Out of Attendance Area requests will not be considered until after the first 10 days of school. Students must be enrolled in their home school for the first 10 days of school. Families will be contacted on day 11, if there is an opening in the requested grade level.
