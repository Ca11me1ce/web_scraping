The Board of Education of Frederick County, Maryland
Frederick Classical Charter School, Inc. (FCCS) is a non-profit organization that operates under contract with the Board of Education (BOE) of Frederick County Public Schools (FCPS) to educate children in Frederick County.
Learn more about the Board of Education at the FCPS website 
here
.
BOE members are elected by voters in Frederick County. FCCS community members greatly appreciate the time offered by these good citizens to help our school succeed.  As a school that strives to develop citizenship in our scholars, we thank you, the BOE, for your service to our community!
