Donate
Thank you in advance for supporting our school! Frederick Classical Charter School, Inc. is registered as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Contributions are tax-deductible to the full extent allowable by law. Throughout the year, the school sponsors fundraising campaigns and events. Please click below to access active campaigns or contribute to the General Fund.
General Contributions
Frederick Classical Charter School is a publicly funded school.  However, funding from the Frederick County Public School system is provided as a standard per-pupil fee.  Additional funding is needed to be able to execute the full vision of the school. Contributions to the General Fund support small and large purchases to meet the needs of the school, including support for student activities, classroom materials, technology, additional staffing support, training, supplies, and other needs of the school. This is the best way to financially support the school, by not limiting the use of funds. To designate specific use of funds, please email finance@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org.
