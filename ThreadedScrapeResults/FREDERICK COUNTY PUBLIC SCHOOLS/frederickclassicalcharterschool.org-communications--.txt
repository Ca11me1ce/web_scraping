Communications
Community Members and Prospective FCCS Families:
FCCS occasionally sends out community announcements about the school, including lottery/admissions information, open parent education nights, charter school advocacy information, and other general communications.  To be added to our general email distribution list, please complete the form, 
Click Here!
Currently Enrolled FCCS Families:
1) Please sign up for 
FindOutFirst
 to receive email communications from the Head of School and emergency announcements.  Set up your subscription for Frederick Classical CS and select all types of communications.  These emails will come from FCPS_FCCS@ezcommunicator.com and other FCPS addresses depending on your subscription settings. 
2) Please complete the form at this link 
http://tinyurl.com/y6whe4uo
 to add your email address on the school's distribution list for other announcements and communications from the Board of Trustees and school committees. These communications will come from Frederick_Classical_Charter_Scho@mail.vresp.com.
Troubleshooting:
If you are not receiving FindOutFirst emails, please first review your FindOutFirst settings to ensure you have set up your "Subscription" to include Frederick_Classical_CS and select all types of announcements.  Some users have reported that their settings appear to have been reset to a default. 
Archived messages are available 
here
.  If you require assistance with school communications, please send an email to the Communications Committee at communications@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org.
