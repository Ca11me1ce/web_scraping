Support the school
Thank you for your interest in supporting Enlace Academy!
There are many ways people can help give to Enlace Academy:
Donate funds
Donate your time and/or services
Donate school items
If you would like to make a financial contribution to Enlace Academy, please mail a check,
made payable to “Enlace Academy” to:
Enlace Academy Charter School
School Leader, Kevin Kubacki
3725 Kiel Ave.
Indianapolis, IN 46224
Enlace Academy Charter School is a 501 (c) 3 organization and all contributions are tax deductible.
Your monetary or in-kind donation will directly impact our academic and social curricula so that our scholars can recognize their potential.
If you are interested in supporting our school in any way and would like more information please email 
info@enlaceacademy.org
Visit
Enlace Academy welcomes visitors.
If you are interested in visiting Enlace Academy, please email us at 
info@enlaceacademy.org
 with information on the nature of your visit. Your email should include the purpose of your visit, your preferred date(s), anticipated guests, and primary contact information. We will do our best to accommodate all requests.
