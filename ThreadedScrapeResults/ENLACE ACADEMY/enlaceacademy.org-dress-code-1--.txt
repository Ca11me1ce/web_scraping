Dress Code
Monday - Thursday
Pants:  Khaki or navy blue dress pants.
Shirt:  White, green, or navy blue polo shirts.  Enlace Academy shirts may be purchased at the office.  You can also purchase Enlace Academy patches for shirts you have already purchased.
Sweaters:  Students may wear a navy blue, green, or white sweater with no writing or pictures.
Sweatshirts:  Students may only wear Enlace Academy sweatshirts, which can be purchased in the office.
Shoes:  Students must wear closed-toe shoes.
Friday
Pants:  Khaki or navy blue pants OR blue jeans .
Shirt:  Enlace Academy t-shirts, which you can purchase in the office OR a white, green, or navy blue polo shirts.  Enlace Academy shirts may be purchased at the office.  You can also purchase Enlace Academy patches for shirts you have already purchased.
Sweaters:  Students may wear a navy blue, green, or white sweater with no writing or pictures.
Sweatshirts:  Students may only wear Enlace Academy sweatshirts that can be purchased in the office.
Shoes:  Students must wear closed-toe shoes.
