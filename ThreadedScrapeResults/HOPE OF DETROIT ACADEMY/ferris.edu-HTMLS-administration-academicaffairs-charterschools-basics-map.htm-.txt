Map of Ferris Charter School Locations
Here you will find information on all of the academies authorized by Ferris State                              University. This area will show you school locations, contact information, board of                              director information, annual reports, charter contracts, and school calendars.                           
Ferris State University currently charters 21 schools geographically dispersed in                              the Lower Peninsula. The location maps on this page will allow you to locate each                              school. For convenience, two maps are provided, one showing the overall State of Michigan                              and detail of the Detroit Metro area.                           
All information in this site is considered public information and may be copied and                              downloaded without penalty. Anyone requesting additional information through a Freedom                              of Information Act (FOIA) request are asked to
