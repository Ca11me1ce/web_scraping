About Shoreline Academy
	Shoreline Academy is a charter school with the Erath Excels Academy School District. Our home office is located in Stephenville, Texas. Our Shoreline campus is located in Taft, Texas. We are committed to providing a quality education to all students. Our campus utilizes online curriculum. Each student is assigned a homeroom and online courses. There, a teacher guides students through their coursework, offers assistance and tutoring, grade assignments, and offers feedback.
Serves grades 7-12
Low teacher to student ratio
Credit recovery
High School Equivalency Program (HSEP) for eligible students
Special Education/ESL services
Curriculum aligned with TEA requirements
Computer skills/use of technology
Online learning curriculum
