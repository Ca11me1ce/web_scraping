Employment Opportunities
Currently Bridgewater Academy is fully staffed, however, we are taking applications for the following position(s):
Math Teacher
Cafeteria Workers
Substitute Teachers
All qualified teaching applicants will possess the following:
• Minimum of Bachelor’s Degree in subject area
• Valid South Carolina certification in subject area/ grade level (
Dual Certification is highly favored.)
• SLED clearance
• Experience preferred
Ideal candidates will also demonstrate ability in:
• planning and developing curriculum for diverse population of students
• organizational and classroom management skills
• positive interpersonal skills
• effective communication skills
• willingness to create and expand classroom instruction to a global scope
We proudly serve students Kindergarten through 8th grade, and welcome all interested parties to apply. Please send cover letter, resume, a copy of certification along with three letters of reference to wilson@bridgewateracademy.org or Bridgewater Academy, 191 River Landing Blvd, Myrtle Beach, SC 29579, or fax to (843) 236-4921. Applications may also be obtained from the main office Monday – Friday from 8 AM until 4 PM.
