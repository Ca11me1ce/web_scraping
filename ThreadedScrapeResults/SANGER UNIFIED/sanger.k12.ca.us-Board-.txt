Meeting Schedule
Regularly scheduled meetings of the Sanger Unified School District Board of Trustees are held at 7:00 p.m. on the second and fourth Tuesday of each month in the District Administration Office Board Room,1905 Seventh Street, Sanger, CA 93657.
The agenda for a regularly scheduled meeting of the Board of Trustees is posted the Friday prior to a Tuesday meeting.
