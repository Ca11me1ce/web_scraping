Education:
Ken graduated with a Bachelor’s Degree in Biology from the University of Wisconsin-Oshkosh. 
Experience:
Ken has had many years of working in the school setting. His experiences include:  working as a teacher’s aide, tutoring struggling students, and promoting reading as a Reading Specialist.  Ken began his career at Noble Academy in January of 2012.
Reason for working in Education:
Ken began his career in a school because of his passion for reading and his enthusiasm to work with students.  Since becoming a library technician, it has been a blessing for him because he is able to share his love of books with the students.   It has also been an absolute joy and honor for Ken to see the student’s reading skills grow.
Interesting Facts and Hobbies:
Ken enjoys playing sports, trying out different exercise programs, drawing, listening to music, and most of all, catching students with overdue library books.
