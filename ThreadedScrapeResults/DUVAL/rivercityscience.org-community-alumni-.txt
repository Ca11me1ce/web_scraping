River City Science Academy Alumni 
Students do not say goodbye to RCSA on graduation day. RCSA offers continuous support to alumni as they enter college. RCSA staff help alumni transition to college by visiting them. RCSA also seeks to help alumni connect to one another. By building a strong network, RCSA alumni can serve as invaluable resources to each other in college and in the future. RCSA also provides resources to alumni as they seek employment during and after college. An alumni new graduate membership is free for first 4 years. A single full or associate membership costs $25 annually after first 4 years .
If you are interested in joining the RCSA Alumni Organization, please fill out the contact form here.
