We Need Your Pictures
October 10, 2017
Yearbooks are no fun without pictures, so we need your photographs!  Please share all of your fabulous Magellan pictures!
No need to wait until spring to send in those pretty pictures, upload them now! To upload your pictures, please go to the Magellan website and click on the “
Lifetouch Yearbook Community Upload”
 link. The code is DAUKOW (case sensitive).
You can also send in a disc with pictures.  Please indicate the grade level of the students photographed.  If you only have a couple photographs, you can email them to me as well.
Please contact  
Heidi High
t
  if you have any questions and thank you for your pictures!
