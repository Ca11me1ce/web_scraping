Westlake Charter School accepts a wide variety of donations to help offset costs for field trips, supplies, and more. Donations made to the school are voluntary as well as tax-deductible, as Westlake Charter School operates as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization (Westlake Charter School’s Tax ID # is 32-0149236). If you are interested in donating to the school, please feel free to do so below.
