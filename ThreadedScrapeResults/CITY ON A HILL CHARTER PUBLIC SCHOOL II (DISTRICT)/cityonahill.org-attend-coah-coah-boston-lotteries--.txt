CoaH in Boston
City on a Hill in Boston
City on a Hill has 2 campuses in Boston – CoaH Circuit Street and CoaH Dudley Square. They have separate admissions processes and maintain separate waitlists. We encourage students who are interested in attending City on a Hill in Boston to enter the lottery for both schools.
2018-2019 school year! 
The main lottery for the 2018-19 school year took place on March 9th, 2018.
There will be another lottery on May 14th, 2018.  Enter a lottery
 form
 by May 13, 2018 at 11:59 PM to be included.
Enter the lottery
Upcoming Admitted Students Day:
Circuit Street:
March 29, 2018 – 5 pm to 6:30 pm – 58 Circuit Street, Roxbury, MA
No RSVP required.
Dudley Square:
April 3, 2018 – 5:30 pm to 7 pm – 2179 Washington Street, Roxbury, MA
No RSVP required.
