Our Students
The New Bedford student body is made up of a variety of ages, ethnicities, learning levels and populations. We highly value our school’s diversity.
Testimonials
“I love the friendly atmosphere at City on a Hill and I love how much help I get from all the teachers when I need it.” – Alexis, Class of 2018
“What I like most about CoaH is that each and every teacher, principal, tutor, and staff is there to help you with every problem you may encounter.” – Ja’vaun, Class of 2019
“What I like most are the opportunities I’m able to have, such as working for the school and being a mentor to new arriving students. With the support of CoaH staff and the education available to me, I know I will be able to achieve my goals and become successful!” – Jasmine, Class of 2018
