Career Opportunities
City on a Hill is hiring!
City on a Hill is nationally recognized as an innovative educational program serving low-income, urban families. Join our winning network of collegial educators and administrators on three campuses that aim to close the achievement gap in grades 9-12 and attain 100 percent college completion.
Current Openings
For a complete list of our job openings and to apply, please visit our careers page below.
CURRENT JOB OPENINGS
City on a Hill is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage applicants of all race, ethnicity, and backgrounds to apply. Thank you again for your interest in City on a Hill! 
No phone calls or faxes, please.

Urban Fellowship Program
Learn about our one year, on-site licensure program focused on training effective teachers in public urban high schools.
CoaHCORPS Tutorial Program
Interested in a year of service (Aug. – Aug) working in an urban charter high school? Join our team of CoahCORPS tutors!
