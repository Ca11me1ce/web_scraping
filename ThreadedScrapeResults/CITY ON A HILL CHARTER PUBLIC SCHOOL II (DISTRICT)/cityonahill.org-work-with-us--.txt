Work With Us
Join our network of talented educators and administrators!
City on a Hill Charter Public Schools is looking for dedicated teachers, administrators, and network team leaders to join our talented staff.
City on a Hill is a network of three thriving, diverse college preparatory high schools aiming to close the achievement gap among urban students. We’re currently seeking applicants for positions at all three schools. Join our winning network of collegial educators and administrators on one of our campuses: City on a Hill Circuit Street (Boston), City on a Hill Dudley Square (Boston), City on a Hill New Bedford.
