Financial Aid Counseling
In Boston, City on a Hill partners with 
uAspire
 to receive individualized financial aid counseling for each senior.  In New Bedford, City on a Hill students will receive equally effective financial aid resources either by partnering with a outside organization or by providing them in-house.
For more information about City on a Hill’s College Prep Program, please contact our Director of College Counseling, 
Natasha deSherbinin
, or your student’s school’s college counselor:
