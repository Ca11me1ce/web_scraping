Admissions Policy
Admissions Policy
City on a Hill schools are public charter schools and are open to all students. Admission to City on a Hill is non-selective and non-discriminatory and is won by lottery. There are no admissions exams. We welcome students regardless of race, color, national origin, creed, sex, gender identity, ethnicity, sexual orientation, mental or physical disability, age, ancestry, athletic performance, special need, proficiency in the English language or a foreign language, or prior academic achievement.  Preference is given to siblings of currently enrolled students.
