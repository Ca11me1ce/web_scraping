Attend City on a Hill Dudley Square
Complete a Lottery Entry Form
City on a Hill only admits students who are entering grade 9. Preference in the lottery is given to siblings of current students and residents of Boston.
2018-2019 school year
The main lottery for the 2018-19 school year took place on March 9th, 2018.
There will be another lottery on May 14th, 2018.  Enter a lottery
 form
 by May 13, 2018 at 11:59 PM to be included.
Enter Online
If you were selected in this lottery round, come by for a visit at the upcoming admitted students day:
April 3, 2018 – 5:30 pm to 7 pm – 2179 Washington Street, Roxbury, MA
No RSVP required.
If you prefer to apply via hard copy:
Print
 entry form and mail to: Admissions, 15 Allerton Street, Suite 1, Roxbury, MA 02119
