Our School
Live Oak is a Waldorf inspired public charter school serving families in Sonoma and Marin counties. Our program is designed to support the holistic development of our students throughout their childhood, from Kindergarten through eighth grade. 
Live Oak offers a classical liberal arts program that honors the creative and intellectual gifts of our children. Through Interdisciplinary scholarship, creative arts, and a positive school culture, our students are prepared for high school with confidence, proficiency, and imagination.
Our Website
Please take a moment to explore our site. If you are new to Waldorf please visit the Education page to learn more about our developmental model and pedagogy.

The Leaflet
The Leaflet is the weekly newsletter for Live Oak Charter School.  The Leaflet is the main source of information and news to keep parents and the community up to date on issues and events at the school.  Articles on Waldorf Education, Parenting, and guest pieces from parents and staff are often featured.  School Board updates on actions currently before the board as well as information interpreting policy additions and changes are often included.  Archived Copies of the Leaflet are available below.
Submissions
If you have an article about school related events or would like to share an appreciation with the Live Oak Community , please email the director at 
director@liveoakcharter.org
.   
Distribution
The leaflet is available in paper form at both the upper and lower campuses.  It is also published as a blog at 
liveoakleaflet.blogspot.com
.  You may visit the blog via the web, or subscribe to the Lealfet to receive the articles as they are published via email.  The subscription button is to the right.
Archive
The Leaflet is archived weekly as a PDF document and posted below.
