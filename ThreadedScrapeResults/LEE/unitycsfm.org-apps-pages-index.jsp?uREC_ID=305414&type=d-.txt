												Principal's Message									
Dear Families,
We are pleased that you have taken the time to visit the Unity Charter School of Fort Myers website.  Our hope is that you find our website informative and user-friendly. After your visit, it is our hope, that you will have a good idea of all we have to offer.
Unity Charter School is a great place for students from diverse backgrounds to grow educationally, get involved, and have a meaningful school experience.  Our school is on its way to becoming THE K-8 school of excellence in all of Lee County.
Whether it is academically or athletically, Unity Charter School of Fort Myers will excel.
We strive to create an environment where each and every student is given the attention necessary to achieve his/her individual success.
All of this is made possible by strong partnerships that are developed with our parents.  I hope that our website will assist in the endeavor.  As always, if you have questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.
Go Eagles!
   George E. Coates, M.Ed.
