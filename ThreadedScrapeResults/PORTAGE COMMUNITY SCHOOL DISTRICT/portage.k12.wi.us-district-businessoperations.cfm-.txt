Business Operations
Welcome to the Portage Community School District's Business Office webpage. The Business Office is responsible for school district budgeting and finance, transportation, food services, purchasing, accounts payable, accounts receivable, building and grounds, and other auxiliary services. The goal of the Business Office is to provide the most efficient services as possible to support the educational objectives of the Portage Community School District. Please feel free to contact me if I can be of service to you.
Margaret Rudolph
Director of Business Operations
