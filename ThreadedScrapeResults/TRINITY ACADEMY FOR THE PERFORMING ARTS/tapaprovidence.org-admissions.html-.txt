Admissions
TAPA is a 7 - 12 public charter school for students living in the city of Providence with a passion for the performing arts. 
Students are admitted to TAPA by lottery. Families wishing to be entered into the lottery should complete an admissions application. Applications may be completed online using the link below, at an open house or by visiting the school's main office.
We encourage all interested families to attend an open house event prior to the lottery.
For questions about admissions or wait list process, please contact Zuleika Vidal, Admissions Coordinator, (401) 644-7657, 
ms.vidal@tapaprovidence.org
