PAST TAPA EVENTS
TAPA’s production of Benches: A Night of One Act Plays is now on sale! Tickets are $10 in advance and there are only 40 seats available for each show, so tickets will sell out fast. Come see these powerful and thought-provoking plays on December 14th & 15th. The shows start promptly at 7:00pm.
Don't wait to buy tickets, or you may miss out. No tickets will be sold at the door.
Contact Mr. White, Theater Artist in Residence, for ticket purchase and production information: 
mr.white@tapaprovidence.org
, (401) 432-7881.

TRY OUT A NEW UNIFORM DAY
Every Friday in December will be "try a new uniform day" in honor of the winning advisories of DEDA (Drop Everything and Do Arts). We hope all students will participate and enjoy the uniform change up on the designated Fridays. Students choosing not to participate must wear the regular school uniform.
Friday, December 8th
Thanks to Ms. Cortese and her advisory The Golden Chords, the uniform for the day will be black top and jeans.
Friday, December 15th
Thanks to Ms. Nadeau and the LOTR advisory, the uniform for the day for for Upper School students will be dress in the color of your arts major:
Music wears Pink
Theater wears Blue
Film wears Purple
Dance wears Red
 Thanks to Ms. Paquette and her advisory the Mockingjays, Lower School students will dress to match their house:
7A wears maroon and white
7B wears navy blue with yellow ducks if possible
8A wears black with red, purple, green or blue
8B wears black and white
Friday December 22nd
Thanks to Ms. Cruz and her advisory the Firecrackers, the uniform of the day will be anything but the uniform, so this one is a dress down day. Enjoy!!
