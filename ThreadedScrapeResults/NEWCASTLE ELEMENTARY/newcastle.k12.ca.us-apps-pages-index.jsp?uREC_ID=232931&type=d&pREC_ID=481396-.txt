Human Resources
If you are interested in employment with the Newcastle Elementary School District, please fill out an application. Applications may be submitted directly to the school site or to the District Office at 450 Main Street, Newcastle, CA 95658.
TEACHER SUBSTITUTES: We are always looking for well-qualified substitutes. To apply, please submit a completed Certificated Application to the District Office along with a copy of your driver's license, TB test clearance, Social Security Card and a CBEST certificate or credential. All our Substitute Teachers must be enrolled in PCOE's Fingerprint Consortium. (PCOE Fingerprint Consortium 530-889-5993).
Application
CLASSIFIED SUBSTITUTES: If you are interested in being hired to substitute for classified positions including Instructional Assistants, Campus Supervisors, Custodians, etc., please complete the Classified Application below and return it to the school site or the District Office 
along with a copy of your driver's license, TB test clearance and Social Security 
Card. All Classified Substitutes must be fingerprinted through the District and receive clearance before employment begins.
Application
Thank you for your interest in working for the Newcastle Elementary School District. If you have any questions, please call the District Office at 916 259-2832, extension 200.
