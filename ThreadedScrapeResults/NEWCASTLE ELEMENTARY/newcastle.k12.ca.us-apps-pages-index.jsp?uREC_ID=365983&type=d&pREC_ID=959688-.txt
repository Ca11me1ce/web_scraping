Mission Statement
The Maintenance and Custodial Department of Newcastle Elementary School District is devoted to the principals of customer service and to providing a safe and healthy environment that is conducive to our students’ ability to learn and excel in their education. Our objective is to provide comprehensive, on-going maintenance, repair and support as well as identifying, budgeting, scheduling and implementing effective work strategies that ensure that the Newcastle Elementary School District standards are being achieved at all of our sites. The Maintenance and Custodial Department is happy to do its part to contribute to the District goal of providing a learning environment for “Creating Extraordinary Futures” for all of our students.
