Located on the Chicago Park Elementary School campus and serving our community for 19 years. Our school offers easy transition for preschoolers to enter into Kindergarten and we have easy access right off Highway 174 for our families. We offer a warm, friendly, positive environment encouraging children to explore and grow at their own pace. We have a dedicated staff that believes in supporting our students, families and Chicago Park School.
