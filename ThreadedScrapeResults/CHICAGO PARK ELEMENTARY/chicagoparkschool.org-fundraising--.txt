Our School Needs Your Support!
If you would like to make an online donation to our fantastic school, please click on a payment option below. All donations are tax deductible and will go to support our enrichment programs. The Chicago Park PTA is a 501(c)(3) Non-Profit organization: EIN is 90-0420056.
The Support Chicago Park Campaign’s goal is to raise $6,000 all of it going to enhance our children’s education thru Music, Sport, Arts, Teachers’ financial support, etc. We encourage you to read the Principal Letter, the PTA Letter, and to download and share the Donation Form with your family/friends who might be willing to support our school. Thank you!

We need your help! Chicago Park is earning easy money by participating in this wonderful program.  There are five ways to earn, but the easiest is to simply clip your box tops, put them in a baggie with your child’s name on it, and dropping them off at school. Learn more:

Fundraising is easy with eScrip! Participating business partners contribute a percentage of your grocery loyalty cards, credit card, and debit/ATM card purchases to our school.  To visit their website, register, and view a complete list of participating merchants click the link below.
