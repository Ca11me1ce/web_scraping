Our Values
Ashe Students are A.C.E.S.
Achievement 
Accomplishing meaningful goals through consistent effort, perseverance, and reflection. 
Community 
A supportive and caring group that takes ownership of creating a positive environment in our school and achieving our mission.
Excellence
We lead in ways that are worthy of being imitated.
Self 
Know who you are and believe there is always room for improvement.  
Adventures
ESYNOLA
ESYNOLA is a FirstLine Schools signature program that changes the way children eat, learn, and live. Ashe features an ESYNOLA garden and teaching kitchen where students participate in gardening and culinary classes.
Visit 
www.esynola.org
 for more information.
Athletics
Ashe is a member of the New Orleans Charter School Athletic Association. Ashe students, grades 5 through 8, can compete in flag football, baseball, basketball, volleyball, and softball.
Enrichment
Ashe offers visual art, dance, band, running club, yoga, and tennis club.
Experiences
Ashe creates unique opportunities that broaden the horizons of its students with local and out-of-town trips and cultural experiences, including theatrical performances, museums, wilderness retreats, and college campus visits.
