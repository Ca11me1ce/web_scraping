Good afternoon MMCA Families,
Here is the link to our April 2018 Newsletter: 
http://docs.wixstatic.com/ugd/4b8266_e5baaca9b2eb4e1a8d8f1e18d9eab35f.pdf
Additionally, every month a specific class at MMCA is responsible for hosting/ working in the Garden for a workday. This month it is Children’s House turn to host our garden work afternoon. This great volunteer opportunity is available on Wednesday, April 18 from 2:00-4:00p.  We are offering double volunteer hours for this event. We encourage the children to join us with digging, weeding and watering our outdoor classroom.
Please sign up in the office or our classroom for this spring time activity.
Thank you!
