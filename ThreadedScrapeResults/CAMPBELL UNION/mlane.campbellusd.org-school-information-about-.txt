About Marshall Lane
Video of MarshallLaneES HD
Marshall Lane is located at the southern end of the Campbell School District, in the city of Saratoga. Its peaceful setting creates a safe and protected environment for students.
At Marshall Lane, we emphasize academic achievement within a nurturing environment. Our success is attributed to a dedicated and hard-working staff, a well-rounded challenging curriculum, active parental involvement, and a community devoted to supporting education. In 2004, Marshall Lane was, once again, proud to be chosen as a California Distinguished School. In 2005, Marshall Lane was honored to be named a National Blue Ribbon School.
School Highlights:
Excellent program focused on helping every student succeed
Strong parental involvement and a supportive community
Art, music, and physical education enrichment programs
Reading intervention for struggling readers
Before and after school childcare
Preschool program
Technology integration
After school art, music, sports, and theater arts
PTA sponsored events
Student recognition programs
Mission Statement
Marshall Lane is a community that is committed to inspiring and empowering life-long learners who possess the mindset and skills to thrive in a changing world.
