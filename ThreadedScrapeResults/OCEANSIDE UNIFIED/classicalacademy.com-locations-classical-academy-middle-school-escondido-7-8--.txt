Welcome
Classical Academy Middle School (CAMS) provides 7th and 8th grade students a well-rounded middle school experience based on a unique blend of classroom instruction supported by credentialed teachers and independent study overseen by a parent educator at home.
CAMS partners with parents to provide a blended learning environment that allows students to maximize potential through offering academic and co-curricular choices, fostering creativity, and developing self-directed, lifelong learners. With blended learning and elective options available, Classical Academy Middle School inspires students with a challenging, supportive, and motivating program that allows them to grow and thrive!
Message from the Principal, Jennifer Morrow
Thank you for your interest in Classical Academy Middle School (CAMS). Go Badgers!
My name is Jennifer Morrow, and as your principal, I am pleased to welcome you to our school. Classical Academy Middle School is a dynamic, tuition-free public charter school of choice serving grades 7 and 8. Classical Academy Middle School is passionate about providing the best education possible for students to become successful thinkers, communicators, and achievers. Our mission is to partner with parents to create self-directed, passionate learners equipped with the skills and strategies to think, communicate and achieve in middle school, high school and beyond. Our school offers a nurturing environment with a commitment to high academic standards and strong moral character.
CAMS provides a positive culture, rich with options for students to challenge themselves socially, artistically and athletically. Students have the opportunity to develop character and confidence as they reinvent possibilities for the future.
The success of our program is based on meeting the developmental needs of our students, offering the best curriculum options, hiring the most passionate teachers, and most importantly, nurturing a strong partnership with parents.
I look forward to the opportunity to serve you and your student! If you are interested in learning more about our schools or our award winning programs, pleases contact the front office at 760-520-6800.
See you on campus!
Jennifer Morrow
