Family Engagement Center
Family Engagement Center at DRIHSCS
The Family Engagement Center hosts several events and workshops throughout the year such as Job Fairs, Resume Writing, Nutrition just to name a few. The Family Engagement Theory is parent involvement closes the achievement gap.
Please look for upcoming Monthly Calendar Events  on our front page
Parents, come to our workshops to learn the following information:
How your child is learning in their different subject areas
How we are supporting and preparing your child for the state exams
How you can support their academics at home and so much more…
Padres, asistan a nuestros talleres para aprender la siguiente información:
Cómo aprende su hijo en sus diferentes materias
Cómo apoyamos y preparamos a su hijo para los exámenes estatales
Cómo usted puede apoyar su progreso académico en casa y mucho más…
