First Annual Gala: November 2nd, 2017
October 1, 2017
 in 
Board of Trustees
, 
News & Updates
, 
slideshow
November 2nd is the First Annual DRIHSCS Gala at the Split Rock Golf Course. It will highlight the achievements of our scholars and honor our Institutional Partner, 
Urban Health Plan
, our founder Dr. Richard Izquierdo and Russell Goings who has enriched our school through loaning his amazing art collection and through donating his time as a mentor.  The proceeds of the Gala will benefit our scholars and will raise money for our scholarship fund and college costs. Please reach out to Mr. Christopher Sharpe to reserve your ticket 
csharpe@drihscs.org
 or 
CLICK HERE
 to buy online.
