 Theater After School
Minneola Elementary is proud to present Theater After School at 7pm with a production of Aladdin Jr. This program is available to 3rd, 4th and 5th grade students. Participation is contingent on auditions, attendance, and grades (must maintain a 70 or higher). Transportation is not provided by the school. The cost of this program in $25.  This fee helps to pay for the scipts, costumes, props, and set supplies. Click here for the club application: 
Theater Club Applicaton.
Rehearsals will be Tuesdays and Thursdays from 3:30-5:00pm. Pick up will be at the back car rider line.  Please be prompt.  All students should bring their script, a pencil, and a highlighter to each rehearsal.  Comfortable clothing and sneakers or jazz shoes are required for rehearsals as they will be learning choreography.  For performances, all girls will be required to wear black or nude jazz shoes or ballet slippers.  Girls will also be required to provide nude colored stockings or tights.  Boys will be required to wear black or nude jazz shoes or solid black sneakers (no logos or designs). 
All students who audition will be cast in the show.  Some students will be asked to call backs to audition for specific parts.  However, 
call backs do not garantee a solo or named part.  The same may apply if a student is not called back.  It does not mean they do not have a solo or a named part.  The purpose of call backs is to give the directors another opportunity to see certain students.
