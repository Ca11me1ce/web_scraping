Treadway's Vision:
Treadway Elementary School believes in growth for all students. We do this by developing the academic skills, habits of mind, and character traits necessary to complete the next step in their educational journey.
Treadway's Mission:
Our school is a culturally diverse community of students, parents, and staff, dedicated to creating a safe environment in which each person is treated with unconditional positive regard and acceptance.  All students will be empowered and inspired to reach their full academic potential in preparation of college and career readiness.

The tardy bell rings at 8:25a.m. each morning.  Students need to arrive at school and have enough time to get to class and be seated by 8:25a.m.
The Drop-Off loops/Gates will be closed at 8:25 promptly each morning. Once the gates are locked, parents must bring their child/children into the school office to sign in.
