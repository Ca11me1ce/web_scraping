Welcome
Welcome to Spring Creek Charter School
Nestled in the Ocala National Forest, within the community of Paisley, Spring Creek Charter School serves students in grades Prekindergarten through 8th Grade. We have facilities to serve 700 students. Our teachers and staff are dedicated to the mission and vision of our school. Using Responsive Classroom as our social literacy approach, we are empowering our students to become productive members of our society. SCCS offers many unique opportunities to our students... band, chorus, K-Kids, BUG Honor Roll, STEM classes, Techno Tribe FLL Robotics Team,  as well as after school tutoring. 
 This year our school day begins at 8:00 AM for students receiving breakfast,  and ends at 3:20 PM.  Students are tardy after 8:30 AM.  Wednesdays are early release days, with students being released at 2:20 PM.
