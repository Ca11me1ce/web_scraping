Sophomore Class: $15, includes a class of 2020 shirt.  Students pay Mr. Deck in room 227.
Freshman Class: $10, includes Class of 2021 shirt. Can be paid in room 114, Mrs. Brown.

FREE OFFICE 365 FOR STUDENTS
Each student on our campus has a free Office 365, which entitles them to a copy of the Microsoft Office Software.  Please follow these instructions to install Microsoft Office onto personal devices:
1.) Click 
HERE
 and log-on with your school e-mail account and password.  
        a.) 
E-mail Address: LastName.FirstName@student.lake.k12.fl.us 
                    *(Last name up to 10 characters, First name up to 6)
        b.) 
Password: Student ID Number
2.) Browse the homescreen until you see the tiles "Mail", "Calendar", "People", etc.  
3.) Below the tiles is the link "Install Office On More Devices", click the "Install" link under that.  
4.) You can then choose from the available software options: Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.  Simply click the "Install" box at the bottom.
