Welcome to Camden Academy Charter High School
The purpose of Camden Academy Charter High School is to provide high school age students in the City of Camden with a successful educational experience. The school will do so by promoting interest within an individual’s own education and understanding of a platform for societal participation.
Camden Academy Charter High School curriculum is anchored by the New Jersey Core Curriculum Content Standards and is delivered in a collegiate Academy style.
