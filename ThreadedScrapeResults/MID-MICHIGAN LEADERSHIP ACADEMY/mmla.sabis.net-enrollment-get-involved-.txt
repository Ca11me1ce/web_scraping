                            Why MMLA?
We believe that education can change the world.
We give our students an education that helps them achieve success as the world changes.
Our students develop a solid foundation of knowledge and skills, a love of lifelong learning, and the ability to adapt to change.
Do you want your child to change the world?
Why choose MMLA?
MMLA is a tuition-free, college-preparatory public school academy serving students in the Lansing community.
Curriculum focused on math and English
Positive, diverse learning environment  
Continued assessment of learning
Leadership development through the Student Life Organization
Important Details
Consistent schedule: No late-start Wednesdays 
Transportation available
After school program including Boys and Girls Club
Free breakfast, lunch, & a healthy snack (CEP)
Clear code of conduct and school uniforms
