            				Welcome to The Mid-Michigan Leadership Academy!					
The Mid-Michigan Leadership Academy is a highly regarded institution that provides a high-quality education to a diverse population in the Lansing area. MMLA strives to help all students to achieve their full potential, prepare them for success in college, equip them with the ability and desire for lifelong learning, and strengthen their civic, ethical, and moral values. The school maintains high standards of efficiency and accountability throughout its operation.
