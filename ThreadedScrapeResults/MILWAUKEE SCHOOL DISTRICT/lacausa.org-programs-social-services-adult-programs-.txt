Who We Are
La Causa Social Services Adult Programs
La Causa, Inc.’s Social Services Adult programs provide a vast array of supportive, recovery and strength-based services to adults and families throughout Milwaukee County. We provide services to those who experience mental health, substance abuse, co-occurring mental health and substance use, and individualized life needs.
We offer the following:
Supportive staff, which may include Certified Peer Specialists who have been trained to effectively share their own life experiences thru the recovery process
Experienced clinicians
Emergency crisis support
Licensed bilingual outpatient mental health clinic
Free walk-in clinic offering mental health and substance abuse screening
Positive engagement opportunities
