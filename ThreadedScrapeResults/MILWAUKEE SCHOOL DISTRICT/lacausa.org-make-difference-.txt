How You Can Help
Your support makes our work at La Causa, Inc. possible. You can make a tax-deductible contribution today through our secure donation website, or call Ellen Kendall, director of community affairs and fund development, at (414) 316-5498.
All donations make a difference.
Make a monthly sustaining or one-time gift online.There are many ways you can support the programs and services at La Causa, Inc. and help families in our community. Here are just a few ways to give:
Make a gift in honor or memory of someone special
Include us in your will or estate plan
Donate to our wish list
Increase your gift through an employer matching gift program
If you prefer to make your contribution by mail, please make your check payable to La Causa, Inc., and send to:
La Causa, Inc.
Attn: Development Office
PO Box 04188
Milwaukee, WI 53214
Become a Volunteer: Volunteers make the difference in the lives of the families we serve by cooking meals, holding donation drives, reading to children, and for helping in one of our facilities. If you are interested in volunteer or internship opportunities, contact Ellen Kendall, director of community affairs and fund development, at (414) 316-5498.
Donor Privacy Policy
La Causa, Inc.'s policy is to respect the privacy of our donors. The agency NEVER sells or trades information about its contributors to any other organization or person. Your gift information is known only to you and certain staff members, to the extent necessary to process your gift and send related correspondence. Donor information (name, address, phone, etc.) is entered and maintained in secure software only accessible to key staff. Please feel free to contact our Director of Community Affairs and Fund Development if you have any questions and/or concerns.
