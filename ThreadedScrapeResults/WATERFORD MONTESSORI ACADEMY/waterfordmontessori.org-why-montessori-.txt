Why Montessori?
1.
Hands-on approach to learning for children.
2.    Every child learns at their own pace.
3.    Helps children reach their full potential in all areas of life.
4.    
Helps children become self-motivated, independent, and life-long learners.
5.    Each classroom operates on the principal of freedom within limits.  
6.    Multi-age classrooms allows children to learn in harmony with one another.
7.    Equipment, exercises, and methods to further the child's self-creating processes.
8.    The philosophy of Dr. Maria Montessori aligned to meet with common core state standards.
