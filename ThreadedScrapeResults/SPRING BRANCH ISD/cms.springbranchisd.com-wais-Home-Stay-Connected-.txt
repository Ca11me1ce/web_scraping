Announcements 
You can tune in beginning at 7:30 each day and hear the music.
		Open 
sbvideo.springbranchisd.com
 in a browser.
		You should then see the System Channels page.  From there you will select WAIS.
		After selecting your campus you should see a message stating “The channel requires login” and you will be taken to the login page.  Here you will enter your district email and password and select domain sbs.sbm1
		Once you have logged in you will be taken to the campus channel you selected.  From here you can view the campus “Live Videos” for morning announcements.
