FIELD DAY ALL SCHOOL EVENT 
On Friday, June 1st, SHAA will be holding their annual FIELD DAY! This is a wonderful celebration that rewards our students with STAR Behavior/Attendance and we hope you can join us! Starting April 2nd to May 31st students behavior will be tracked using our PBIS referral system. As of April 2nd all classroom and office referrals will be counted against Field Day. Parents please be advise that absences and no uniforms will be considered a referral. This event will take place on the field directly behind the school and will also be open to SHAA FAMILIES ONLY. 
As a reminder of participation requirements, students must meet the following criteria: 
0-12 referrals: ALL ACCESS to Field Day and Movie
13-15 referrals: Access to Mpvie ONLY 
16 or more referrals: NOT ALLOWED to participate in any activities 
IF your child has shown SUPER S.T.A.R exemplary BEHAVIOR/ATTENDANCE throughout the entire school year, we will recognize them with a special REWARD:
0 absences ALL 2017-18 SCHOOL YEAR (FIELD DAY T-SHIRT)
0-3 referrals ALL 2017-18 SCHOOL YEAR (VIP PASS)
IF your child is participating in Field Day, please be reminded that stuents are required to wear their UNIFORMS and CLOSED-TOED Shoes.
