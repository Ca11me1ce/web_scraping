Welcome
Welcome ! Today is Monday, May 07, 2018.
Welcome to Edgewood Elementary School where o
ur mission is to
 provide students with committed adults who provide rigorous instruction in a positive and caring school climate. In this environment students can achieve at exceptional academic levels by developing critical thinking and problem-solving skills to enhance character development and to improve the community. 
Parents and guardians, please share the following guidelines with your child:
Each scholar is expected to arrive at school in the complete school uniform every day. 
Each scholar is expected to maintain excellent attendance and punctuality. (Remember our goal is 100% attendance.)  
Each scholar must complete assigned class work, homework, and special projects.
As we stress a positive climate, each scholar is expected to display proper behavior and social skills at all times.
