ROBERT W. COLEMAN ELEMENTARY SCHOOL
We are a Title 1 School-wide public school nestled in a Robert Coleman Community of the Greater Mondawmin Area in Baltimore City.  Our school is home to over 300 students in grade pre-kindergarten to fifth grade.   We are excited about moving to full implementation of the common core.  Our promise to you is to prepare your children to be successful learners.   Our school hours are 7:45-2:25PM.  Welcome to Robert W. Coleman Family!
