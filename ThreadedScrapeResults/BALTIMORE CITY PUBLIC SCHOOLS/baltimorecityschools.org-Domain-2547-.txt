Welcome
Greetings all website visitors:
     Welcome into theworld of Westport Academy Elementary/Middle School through our website.Westport Academy is a full Title I school that serves PreK-8th gradestudents from the community of Westport. We are also the home of three Citywideprograms that are designed to educate students with Autism. 
     Nestled in a small, isolated, community insouth Baltimore, we exist to ensure that 
students matriculating here are prepared tobecome productive citizens equipped to meet the demands of our ever-changingsociety.
     Theadministrators, teachers, and support staff are dedicated to ensuring 
allstudents will receive quality instruction, real-world extended learningexperiences, and tailored supports designed to meet each of their diverseneeds. Through the core values of respect, responsibility, and readiness, webelieve our students will be prepared to successfully lead and serve in thegreater community.
    We welcome partnerships, volunteers, anddonations that will support the work we are doing to provide an optimalexperience for our students and families. Should you desire to support us,please call (410) 396-3396/7 to schedule an appointment to meet with Ms. ReneeSweringen, our Family and Community Engagement Chairperson. 
With warmregards,
Melody M. Locke, Principal
Kat Spence, Assistant Principal
