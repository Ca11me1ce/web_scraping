New Arrival and Breakfast Procedures
Starting Wednesday, September 6, 2017, we will be serving breakfast in the cafeteria from 7:25-7:45 a.m. The students should enter through the cafeteria entrance on Arydale Ave. Breakfast will not be served after 7:40 a.m.  After 7:45 a.m. students should enter the campus using the Fernhill Ave.  entrance (main entrance).
Comments (-1)
Visitor Policy
To ensure the safety of all students, no visitor will be admitted in the building between 2:00-2:25 p.m. Upon immediate entry, ALL visitors must sign-in in the office prior to visiting any part of the building.
Comments (-1)
Early Dismissal Policy
All students must be signed out in the office prior to 2 p.m. No student will be released after 2 p.m.
Comments (-1)
Dismissal
All students will be dismissed at 2:25 pm daily. Students in grades Pre-K and K will be dismissed from the gymnasium.  Students in grades 1 – 5 will be dismissed from the blacktop exit facing Callaway Ave.

Callaway Elementary Family, 
Thanks so much for taking the time to visit our website. Callaway Elementary School is a school-wide Title 1  elementary school serving students in Pre-K to Grade 5. We are a true community school that endeavors to 
be a beacon in the community committed to creating opportunities for all students to learn.  We enhance relationships between the school and community, as we shape responsible citizens, inspire critical thinkers, and equip students with fundamental skills.  Our students will be prepared to serve their community and contribute to the global society.
Please take a few minutes to explore our website to see the opportunities we offer to our students and families.
Mr. 
Miguel Cervantes del Toro, M.Ed, Principal
Mr. John Bullock, M.A, Assistant Principal
