Governance Board
The Idea Governance Board meets on the third Monday of every month at 12:00pm. You are invited to come and sit in on any of our open meetings. Feel free to email the advisors or our current governance board memebers if you have an interest in attending a meeting or if you feel you have something to bring to our governance board table.
