At Northwood Academy Charter School, we recognize how critical our partnership with parents is.  our doors are always open and our staff is always available to work with families.  We strive to make sure that our students recognize we are a united team-together we have them in our hands.  Please continue to support your child and our school by joining our Northwood community. 
Throughout the year, we have many opportunities for parents to volunteer their time, attend school celebrations or participate in a school sponsored event.  Please contact us anytime your have a question, idea or concern.  Together we are stronger!
Community Coordinator
Mrs. Amy Kelly
Mrs. Kelly serves as the Community coordinator for Northwood.  Here role includes assisting volunteers, working with the administrative team to incorporate incentives and grade group events, scheduling assemblies, and coordinating the Service Learning program.  The goal of the Community Coordinator is to create opportunities for our students to learn about their community and the need for their services while incorporating families into those events.  Mrs. Kelly os one of the original staff members, some of you may know her from her previous role as a 4th grade teacher.  She is so pleased to see the evolution of this school into a place where a Community Coordinator position exists.  
Please contact Mrs. Kelly for further information on community events, volunteer work and service learning at 
akelly@northoodcs.org
