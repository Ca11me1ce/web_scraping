Displaying Student Data
DC Prep uses data to celebrate our students' successes and to measure their progress. We post student work in classrooms and on our data walls from which teachers plan their teaching priorities. If you do not wish to have your child's work displayed, please contact 
ParentDataQuestions@dcprep.org
. Thank you.
Asthma and Allergy Action Plans
If your child suffers from asthma and/or allergies, please print and have your doctor* complete the following action plan form(s) and drop them off at the BEC Front Desk:
Asthma Action Plan
Anaphylaxis Action Plan
Food Allergy Action Plan: English Version
Food Allergy Action Plan: Spanish Version
*Please note, these forms are to be filled out by a doctor, only for students diagnosed with asthma and/or a food allergy.
