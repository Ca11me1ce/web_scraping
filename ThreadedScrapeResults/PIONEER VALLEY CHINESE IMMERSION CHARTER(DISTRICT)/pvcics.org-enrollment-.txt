Enrollment
As a tuition-free public charter school, PVCICS is open to all students residing in Massachusetts. Entrance is by lottery.   To enter the lottery, parents submit an enrollment application available below.  
The lottery establishes the order in which students will be accepted into the school. Siblings of currently enrolled students will be given priority over other students in the lottery ranking. Additionally, students from within PVCICS's region of service, as defined in our charter, will be given priority over students outside the region.  
Please see the information session schedule below. Information sessions are not required. Students entering Kindergarten must be 5 years old by September 1st of the year of entry. Entering students must have successfully completed the previous grade in the previous year.  For example, students entering 9th grade must have successfully completed 8th grade in the previous year.

Lottery Deadlines and Dates
For entry in the 2018-2019 school year:
Applications submitted to PVCICS by February 13, 2018, 4:00 PM will participate in the first pre-enrollment lottery, to be held on February 15, 2018 at 4:00 PM at the school. 
Applications submitted after February 13, 2018, 4:00 PM, will participate in subsequent lotteries. Lotteries held after February 15, 2018 will not change the status of students already offered admission to PVCICS, but may change the ranking of students on the waiting list. 
March 10th, 2018 at 4:00 PM is the application deadline for the second lottery that will be conducted on Tuesday March 13th, 2018 at 1:00 PM. 
June 20th, 2018 at 4:00 PM is the application deadline for an additional lottery that will be conducted on Friday June 22, 2018 at 4:00 PM.

Region of Service 
PVCICS can accept applications and enroll Massachusetts resident students from anywhere in the state. However, if there are more applicants than available slots, students residing in the region of service defined in our charter receive enrollment preference.
