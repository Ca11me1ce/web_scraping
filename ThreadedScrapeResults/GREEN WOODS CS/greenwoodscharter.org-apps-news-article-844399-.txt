				GWCS to host inaugural "Miles for Marlee Rae" Walk-A-Thon			
						Read the article to learn more about how to get involved in this amazing event!					
Click here
 to learn more about how to participate in Green Woods Charter inaugural "Miles for Marlee Rae" Walk-A-Thon. 
We are looking for volunteers to help us make this event successful.  Please Follow 
this link
 to sign-up to help!
Overview:
Attire: Gym uniform pants and sneakers required.  Students may wear Marlee gear or a Gray t-shirt (Gray is the color that represents Brain Cancer).
Participants: ALL students, regardless of donations, will participate unless they have a medical condition or a doctor’s note.
Opening Ceremonies  
8:30 - All students report to the gym
8:40 - Pledge of Allegiance
8:42-9:00- Speech from Miss Libby (GW parent whose child has benefited from Lisa’s Army)
9:00 - Grades K-2 reports to the rain garden.  Grades 3-8 returns to classrooms.
9:00 Grades K-2
Warm ups lead by the DJ and warm up instructor (Walk the entire driveway)
Warm up (5 mins)
Walk (20 mins)
finished by 9:30am
9:50 am Grades 6-8 (report to rain garden)
Warm ups lead by the DJ and warm up instructor (Walk the entire driveway)
Warm up (5 mins)
Walk (45 mins)
finished by 10:50am
11:00 am Grades 3-5 (report to rain garden)
Warm ups lead by the DJ and warm up instructor (Walk the entire driveway)
Warm up (5 mins)
Walk (30 mins)
finished by 11:40am
12:25- Closing Ceremonies - Students report to rain garden  (WHOLE SCHOOL)
Marlee’s Mission Speech by a representative from Lisa’s Army
Tree Dedication - Ms Finnin and Miss Gasparino
Student performance - Aurora Karamichalakos  
Check presentation - Ms. Taylor
Additional Information: 
Students who raise $50 will get a T-shirt at a later date.
Parents are welcome to cheer their kids on!
Tables will be set up along the course with water. (NO water bottles needed.)
A Marlee table will be set up the day of walk to purchase gear.
