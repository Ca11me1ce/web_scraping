A Message from our Founders 
 Dr. Gary Short, Superintendent 
“The partnership with Texas A&M San Antonio provides an outstanding
opportunity for SWPS students to begin college life and complete a four year
degree at an earlier stage in their lives.  SWPS believes that this positive
collaborative relationship with Texas A&M San Antonio will stronglyenhance the overall educational environment of our organization and thecommunity at large.
It is an exciting adventure with a win-win focus for everyone involved.”  
~Dr. Gary Short, Superintendent

James Neal
