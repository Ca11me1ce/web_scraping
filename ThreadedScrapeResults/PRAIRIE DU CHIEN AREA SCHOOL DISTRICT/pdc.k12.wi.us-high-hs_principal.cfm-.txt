Prairie du Chien is a comprehensive high school (grades 9-12) with approximately 385 students. The administrative staff consists of Mr. Andy Banasik, Principal and Athletic Director, and Mrs. Karen Sjoberg, Dean of Students and Communications Director.  The faculty at Prairie du Chien numbers 39 with several of the staff having a master degree.
Prairie du Chien High School operates from a 4-Period Day. In this system the students must be in classes 3 to 3 ½ periods. All students, in order to be eligible to participate in the graduation ceremony, must have met all graduation requirements as stipulated. Any student who fails to meet the graduation requirements will not be eligible to participate in the graduation ceremony.
