Superior Preparation for College and Career Readiness
Prairie du Chien High School offers a wide variety of curriculum for the college and career bound student. The goal of PdCHS is to provide every student with an individual plan that fits the career and college goals of the student. Students can choose from 19 Advanced Placement courses, PLTW (Project Lead the Way) courses in the engineering and medical fields, youth apprenticeship, youth options, and a variety of electives. PdCHS students (all are required to participate) score consistently higher than the state average on the ACT.  According to US News, PdCHS is ranked among the best high schools in the nation.  PdCHS students can round out their high school career with many co-curricular activities and clubs.

Mission Statement
The role of the Guidance Office is:
To help students identify educational goals, develop a career plan, and enroll in appropriate courses.
To provide students with information about career training options, school applications, testing, and financial aid opportunities.
Offer individual and group counseling for students dealing with academic problems, relationship issues, and family crisis.
CONFIDENTIALITY
What is confidential?
Federal Law 42 CFR II protects your confidentiality!
- Alcohol use
- Nicotine use
- Drug use
- Curfew violations
- Cutting class
- Sneaking out
- Pregnancy
- Diseases
- Sexual behavior
- Sexual preference
- Your likes/dislikes
- Your opinion of others
What is 
NOT
 confidential?
We are bound by law to report this information:
- If you are suicidal, homicidal, a victim of sexual abuse, a victim of child neglect
- If you have a medical emergency in our presence
