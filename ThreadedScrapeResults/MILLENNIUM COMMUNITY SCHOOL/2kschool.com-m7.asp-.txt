 School Rating
The Millennium Seven (7) 
UNITY 
Demonstrates respect for feelings of others 
Shares with classmates 
Seeks peaceful ways to solve conflicts 
Helps other students with challenging work 
Works well in groups
SELF DETERMINATION 
Remains calm even when criticized or otherwise provoked 
Comes to class prepared 
Pays attention and resists distractions 
Gets to work right away rather than procrastinating 
RESPECT 
Is polite to adults and peers
 Allows others to speak without interruption 
Remembers and follows directions immediately 
Actively listens to others 
GRATITUDE
Recognizes and shows appreciation for others
Takes care of the classroom, school equipment, and personal supplies
Uses manners throughout the day (please, thank you, may I, excuse me, you are welcome)
Takes care of school uniform
PURPOSE
Finishes whatever he or she begins
Asks and answers questions to deepen understanding
Works independently with focus
Actively participates
CREATIVITY
Is eager to explore new things
Finds solutions during conflicts with others
Uses imagination to solve challenging problems
TRUST
Gets over frustrations and setbacks quickly
Tries very hard even after experiencing failure
Shows enthusiasm
Inspires others to do their best
