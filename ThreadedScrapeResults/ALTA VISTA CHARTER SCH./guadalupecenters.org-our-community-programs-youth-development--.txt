Summer Enrichment Program
The Guadalupe Centers Youth Development Program is a set of year-round programs aimed at providing the urban core youth with positive educational and enrichment activities. This programming includes: Life Skills Workshops to promote healthy lifestyles at local middle schools, and after school program providing tutoring and homework support, an academic and rigorous 8 week summer school for elementary and middle school students, and a summer-long weekend diversion program for youth to engage in positive enrichment activities.
