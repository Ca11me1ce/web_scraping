Outpatient Treatment Program
The Guadalupe Centers Substance Abuse Program is a state certified outpatient substance abuse program recognized by the Department of Mental Health, Division of Drug and Alcohol. The first fully bilingual (Spanish/English) program in the State of Missouri was designed to educate the Latino community about the perils of substance abuse by:
Assisting the client in gaining awareness of the impact of their substance abuse
Providing the tools necessary to cope with the risk factors present in their lives
Receiving counseling services that support abstinence and promote changes in other areas affected by their use
Providing supportive recovery services to individuals needing outpatient treatment
Our results
Treatment
47% Abstained from use of mood altering substances
56% Made progress with at least 2 personal treatment goals
Prevention
83% Youth increased awareness of impact of substance use, ability to identify alternatives to use, improvement in healthy coping skills
90% Parents increased knowledge of community resources
Services offered
Bilingual (Spanish/English) supportive recovery services treatment program for adult men and women challenged with alcohol/drug abuse.
