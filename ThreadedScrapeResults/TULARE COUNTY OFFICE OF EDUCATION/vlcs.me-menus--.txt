Online Payment System
Valley Life Charter School and MealTime Online are excited to provide an online account payment system. This system allows you to make secure online payments into your student's school meals, view all transactions, and account balance. 
The MealTime Online Payment website uses the most up-to-date security components available in the banking industry. All data transmitted is encrypted and authenticated every time you use the website. Your credit information in only used to precess your deposit. It is never stored on any website. For each deposit you make you will receive a receipt to print out and an email to verify the deposit. 
Use your student's Student Passcode Notification you can go to 
www.mymealtime.com
, sign in to the website, and add your student to your account. You will then gain access to your student's meal information and be able to make deposits.
