Outpatient Mental Health Services
Lighthouse provides behavioral health and psychiatric services for children, youth and families experiencing mental health challenges in the home, school, or community.
Lighthouse is dedicated to helping children and youth remain safely in their homes or smoothly transition through the juvenile justice or child welfare systems. Staff provides services to youth in their own communities while still addressing complex emotional and behavioral needs.
Services are available seven days a week with flexible hours.
