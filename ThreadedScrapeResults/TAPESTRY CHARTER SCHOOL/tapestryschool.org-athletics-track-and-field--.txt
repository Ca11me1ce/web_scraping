Indoor/Outdoor Track & Field
Tapestry Charter School is an Independent school that participates in a number of track meets throughout Western New York against local high schools.  The past three years, Tapestry has had sectional champions in the long jump, triple jump, 100m, 200m, 4x100m relay and 4x400m relay.  The girl's 4x100m relay team were state champions in 2015 and 2016. In 2016,  Nia Stevens became the first ever individual state champion at Tapestry.
About the Coach 
Dan Tryon has been coaching track and field for the past 15 years and has spent the last four years as Head Track and Field coach at Tapestry. Coach Tryon has been blessed to work with some of the best track athletes in Western New York. While coaching at Amherst High School three girl’s sectional records were broken including; 100m, 200m, and the 4x400m relay.
