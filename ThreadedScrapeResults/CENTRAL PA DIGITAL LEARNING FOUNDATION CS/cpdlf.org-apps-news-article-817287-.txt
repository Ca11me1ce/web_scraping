				Cyber Educational Option			
						Central PA Digital Learning Foundation (CPDLF) Charter School is a cyber educational option in Duncansville, PA. Our CEO, full time staff, Instructors, and Learning Guides  are located in the  Appalachia IU8 Educational Development Center building next to the Foot of Ten Elementary School. We have a large conference room for enrollment days as well as a tutoring room for Learners and their Learning Guides.					
Central PA Digital Learning Foundation (CPDLF) Charter School is a cyber educational option in Duncansville, PA. Our CEO, full time staff, Instructors, and Learning Guides  are located in the  Appalachia IU8 Educational Development Center building next to the Hollidaysburg Elementary School. We have a large conference room for enrollment days as well as a tutoring room for Learner's and their Learning Guides. 
Central PA Digital Learning Foundation is located on the far right side of the Educational Development Center building.  Our entrance is across from the baseball field under the maroon awning.
