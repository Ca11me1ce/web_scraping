Corporate Opportunities
Behind the scenes of Leona schools is a full complement of professional support services provided by corporate offices in Phoenix, Arizona and Okemos, Michigan. Satellite offices are located in Florida, Indiana and Ohio.
Leona's corporate support departments display a dedicated approach that goes well beyond the traditional work day and is built on genuine relationships and the unfaltering commitment to be there whenever needed. Career areas include:
Curriculum & assessment
Special education

Budget & finance
Human resources
Communications & marketing
Compliance
