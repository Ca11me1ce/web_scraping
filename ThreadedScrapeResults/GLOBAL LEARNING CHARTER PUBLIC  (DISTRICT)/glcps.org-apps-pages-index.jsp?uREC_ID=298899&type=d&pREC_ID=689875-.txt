Look Who Believes in the Power of Education!   
Corporate sponsors help GLCPS students reach their educational goals right through their college career. All proceeds raised at the Global Gala go to the Global Scholarship Fund for GLCPS graduating seniors and alumni. Scholarship dollars are essential for our students as they tackle the increasing cost of higher education.
