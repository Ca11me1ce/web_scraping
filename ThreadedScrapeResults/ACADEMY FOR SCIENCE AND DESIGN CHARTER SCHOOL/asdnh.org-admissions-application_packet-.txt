Application Packet
Thank you for your interest in the Academy for Science and Design!  
All inquiries should be emailed to 
admissions@asdnh.org
Admissions for 2018-2019 Major Dates:
The Academy for Science and Design began accepting applications for the 2018-2019 school year on September 25, 2017.
The deadline for receipt of completed applications in hand at ASD was 12:00 pm December 11, 2017. 
  Enrollment offers were made in late March (grade 6) and early April (grades 7-11) and we are currently fully enrolled for the 2018-2019 school year with more than 200 applicants remaining on the wait-list.We are no longer accepting any additional applications for 2018-2019.
If you have any questions, please contact Admissions by email at 
admissions@asdnh.org
