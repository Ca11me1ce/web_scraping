WELCOME TO MRS.CHALFIN'S MATH CLASS
2017-18
Cooley Middle School            
Room 207       
Email: 
sallie.chalfin@husd.org
Office hours: Tues. & Thurs. 8:30-8:50 AM
ALL  Information can be found on EDMODO.  
http://husd.edmodo.com/
I post ALL classroom NOTES, Tip sheets, instructional videos & Practice Assessments on my Edmodo page.
ASSESSMENT INFO:
If you need to FINISH or RETAKE an assessment, you must first complete your corrections.  This is completed on lined paper with a detailed explanation of what error(s) you made and then your new work along with a new solution.  Once this has been turned in (along with the original assessment) I will review it to determine if you are ready for a retake.  You will either receive a 'RETAKE' ticket or I will ask you to do some more practice problems before retaking.
Hours to RETAKE:  Tues. or Thurs. - before school 8:15 - 8:45 OR Thurs. after school 4:00 - 4:45 during Tutor Lounge.
Required Class Materials:
Please write CHALFIN on the outside of the supplies 
1.  Fine (Ultra Fine) tip black marker.  This does NOT need to be a Sharpie.
2. 1” three-ring binder - Dividers (will need 3)
3. RECOMMENDED but Not Required:   Plastic Dividers – WITH POCKETS (package of 5) for Organization of Binder
4. 1 Composition Notebook - graph paper recommended but not required
5. Loose Leaf - GRAPH  PAPER
6. Four YELLOW highlighters
7.PENCILS PENCILS PENCILS PENCILS PENCILS PENCILS ….. and more PENCILS J
8. Two Hand Held Pencil Sharpeners
9. 3 Erasers
10. Glue Sticks - 3 please
11. TWO Packages of Dry Erase Markers - MUST MUST MUST MUST be LOW ODOR please.  J Allergies
12.  Box of Kleenex
ADDITIONAL Supplies WISH LIST but not required:
White Copy Paper
Colored Copy Paper
Paper Towels
Individually Wrapped Candy
