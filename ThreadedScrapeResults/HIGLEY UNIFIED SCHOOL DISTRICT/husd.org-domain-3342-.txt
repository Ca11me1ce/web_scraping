Welcome to 8th Social Studies!!!!
  Most importantly welcome back to school.
  This school year will be educational and fun.
  My promise first and formost is to create a caring, close-knit classroom environment, which fosters independent thinking skills, learning and growth.
  This year we will explore the study of history through analyzing visuals, direct instruction, informational text, projects and presentation.
Phone: 480.279.8300
Email: Ben.Fiddler@husd.org
Office Hours: Mon-Thurs 8:00am - 8:45am, Fri 8:00 am- 8:35am (will do appointments)
Room 236 
About Me:
Please allow me to introduce myself.  My name is Benjamin Fiddler and it’s an honor to be your teacher.  I am originally from Grand Rapids, Michigan.  Where I attended Western Michigan University and received a Bachelors degree in Secondary History Education and a minor in English.  This is my 7th year teaching and my 4rd in the state of Arizona.  My wife and I have been together since we were freshmen's in high school and have been joined at the hip ever since.  We will have been married 14 years in August.  We have 3 lovely children Breasia (16), Peyton (7), and Gavin (4) who are students in the district as well.
