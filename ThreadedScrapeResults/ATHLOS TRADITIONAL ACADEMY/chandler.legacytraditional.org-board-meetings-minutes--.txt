Board Meetings
Per A.R.S. §38-431.02, all notices of the meetings for Legacy Traditional School governing boards and any of their committees and subcommittees will be posted on 
this website
. Notices will also be posted at the District administrative offices, 3201 S. Gilbert Rd., Bldg. A, Chandler, AZ 85286, which is open to the public between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m. Monday through Friday except legal holidays. Such notices will indicate the date, time, and place of the meeting and will include an agenda.
