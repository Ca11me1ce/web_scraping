Welcome to Legacy Chandler
Legacy Chandler is a campus of champions. At our full-day kindergarten to 8th-grade tuition-free charter school, students learn from highly-qualified staff. Our students and staff have allowed us to consistently maintain an “A” rating from the state of Arizona. We’ve been building future leaders at our campus since 2011.

Life at Legacy Chandler
Legacy Chandler has a tradition of helping students find success since 2011. Our beautiful campus is home to poets, sculptors, geography whizzes, science fair winners, inventors, community volunteers, leaders, athletes, and scholars. Behind all of that is a dedicated staff that cares about preparing our students for life. Come see what makes Legacy Chandler so amazing!

Your child’s success is our success.
We don’t measure our success merely by test scores or grades of our students (although they are important and you’ll find no higher scores anywhere).
