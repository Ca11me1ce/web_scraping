Attention Student-Athletes!
The 2017-18 school year forms and eligibility video will be available on-line starting July 12th. Each student-athlete and parent/guardian are required to watch the eligibility video and sign all required forms in order to participate in Shawano Athletics for the 2017-18 school year.
Each program will host Parent meetings to address sport specific policies and expectations. All required forms must be on file and verified in the High School office before beginning practice! 
Forms and the video link may be found 
here
.
