Lottery Information
Lottery Information
Annual School Lottery 
The online lottery application opened March 19, 2018 for the 2018-2019 school year.
The lottery for each campus will take place on June 15, 2018.
Emails will be sent out for all applicants selected through the lottery process on June 15th.
All lottery applicants will remain on the lottery waiting list for the entire school term.
Submit only one lottery application per student.
Select only one school campus to submit a lottery application.
