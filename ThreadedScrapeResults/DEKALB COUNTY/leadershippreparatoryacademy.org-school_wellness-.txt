Welcome Parents!
Our School Wellness page will provide you with Medical Forms you may need for your child(ren) as well as Monthly Updates regarding Health and 
Safety Promotion/Education.
I encourage you and your scholar to regularly view this page for health tips and much more throughout the school year. Also, if you have any questions regarding your child's health/safety you can email me, LPA's School Health Personnel,  at 
nursecahill.lpa@gmail.com
. 
Thank you for checking out School Wellness and stay tuned for more updates!
