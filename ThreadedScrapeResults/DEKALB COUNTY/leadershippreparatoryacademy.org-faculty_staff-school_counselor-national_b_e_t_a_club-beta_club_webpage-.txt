The National Beta Club is the largest independent, non-profit, educational youth organization in America. And for more than 80 years, it has prepared today's students to be tomorrow's leaders.  The mission of National Beta Club is to promote the ideals of academic achievement, character, service and leadership among elementary and secondary school students. Students who are recommended for the club exhibit academic excellence and leadership ability here at Leadership Preparatory Academy.
