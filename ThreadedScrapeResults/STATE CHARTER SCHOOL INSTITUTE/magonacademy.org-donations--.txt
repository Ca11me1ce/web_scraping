Support Us
As a small school, we look to outside support to help ensure that our program retains a small student-to-teacher ratio and to finance our full-day academic kindergarten program. Of course we could always use monetary help, but we could also use any donations that may enhance our school in any way. Please feel free to visit us at your earliest convenience and I’m sure that we will amaze you. 
Thank you in advance for you support!
