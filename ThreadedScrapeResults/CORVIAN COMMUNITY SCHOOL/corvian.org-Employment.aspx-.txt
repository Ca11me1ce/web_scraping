Corvian Welcomes Teachers With a Passion for Education            
We are looking to hire passionate educators who are seeking to make a difference in the lives of students. Please submit an application along with your resume. All positions require a NC Teaching License unless otherwise noted.
In compliance with federal law, NC Public Schools administers all state-operated educational programs, employment activities and admissions without discrimination because of race, religion, national or ethnic origin, color, age, military service, disability, or gender, except where exemption is appropriate and allowed by law.
In compliance with North Carolina law, Corvian Community School participates in the E-Verify Program to confirm employment eligibility for all new employees        
Corvian Community School currently offers the following benefits for all full-time employees:            
TSERS Retirement:
BCBS Healthcare:
http://statehealthplan.state.nc.us
NCFLEX Benefits:
http://www.oshr.nc.gov/Newsletters/NCFlexEnrollmentGuide2015/index.html
 We are currently seeking talented and enthusiastic educators in the following areas:            
Licensed High School Teachers (grades 9-12) in all subject areas
Licensed High School College Counselor
Licensed Elementary School Teachers (grades K-4)
Licensed Middle School Teachers (grades 5-8) in all subject areas
Teacher Assistants grades K-5
Bus Drivers
Athletics Coaching Staff 
Please complete our employment application and mail along with your resume and cover letter. Please mail or hand deliver to:
Corvian Community School

Charlotte, NC 28269
