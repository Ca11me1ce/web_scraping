Welcome to Eagle Academy!
Eagle Academy is a tuition-free community school serving grades 6-9.  The mission of Eagle Academy, where kids come first, is to partner with the families to transform the learning process with technology-infused projects and focused instructional strategies that promote enthusiastic learners and high student achievement.
​Eagle Academy offers the following:
Challenging curriculum aligned to the Common Core and Ohio Revised Standards
Structured classroom environments
Free breakfast and lunch programs
Technology integration in all classrooms
Variety of extracurricular activities and sports programs
Free transportation
MEETING NOTICE
The Board of Directors recognizes the importance of collaborating in order to help students have good attendance at school.  Therefore, school employees and volunteers, community members and local juvenile court judges, as well as any other stakeholders, are invited to attend the June meeting of the board of directors to collaborate in developing the academy's Attendance, Truancy, and Automatic Withdrawal Policy.
