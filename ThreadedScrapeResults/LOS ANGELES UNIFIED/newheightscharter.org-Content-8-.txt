Welcome    
WELCOME PROSPECTIVE FAMILIES!
We are delighted with your interest in New Heights Charter School and look forward to sharing New Heights’ unique educational programs.
New Heights serves students in South Los Angeles with an enriched program operates from 7:00 am – 6:30 pm each day.   Learning at New Heights is organized around a cohesive set of research-based ideas and practices about teaching and learning.  The dedicated faculty work together to provide an engaging, personalized educational experience for students.
We understand that choosing the right school for your child is an exciting undertaking, but it can also be an overwhelming experience. To make applying to New Heights as simple as possible, we invite you to submit a one-page application.
We are excited to get to know you and for you to get to know us through our information sessions, teas, and 
school events
 that are open to the public.  If you are interested in joining us, please review the Steps to Apply page.
The faculty thanks you for your interest and hopes that you will see New Heights Charter School as an excellent match for your child and family. Please don’t hesitate to contact us with any questions. We’ll see you soon!
