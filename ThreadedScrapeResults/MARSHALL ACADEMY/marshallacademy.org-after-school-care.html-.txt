Marshall Academy Griffon Group
After School Care  The Griffon Group will provide your child with:
After-school supervion 
Light homework help 
Physical activity 
Caring, supportive atmosphere 
 Time: after dismissal to 5:30pm
Cost:
$3.00 per day per child for pick up in the first hour (3:30 - 4:30)
$6.00 per day per child for any pick up after 4:30, up to 5:30
$2.00 additional for pick up between 5:30 - 5:45; every 15 minutes after that is $5
All money is due to be paid the day of service or Friday of the same week.
Contact Lacey Ferro for more information at 269.781.6330
You must pre-register for Griffon Group. This alleviates confusion regarding where students are to report after school.
