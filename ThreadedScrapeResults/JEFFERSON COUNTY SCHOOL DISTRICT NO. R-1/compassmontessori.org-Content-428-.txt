Compass Montessori has two campuses that are governed by one Board of Directors and authorized by Jefferson County Schools. Each campus is a separate entity and has its own separate charter school agreement with Jeffco Public Schools. Jeffco Public Schools provides both Compass schools with all financial services including A/P, cash receipts, payroll, and financial reporting. The links above contain budget and audit information for each Compass campus or direct links to Jeffco Schools website for access to the data or reports listed.
