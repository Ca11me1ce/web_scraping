About Us
Founded in 1998, Summit High School is an alternative public charter school dedicated to helping students achieve the goal of high school graduation by offering:
Caring, highly qualified teachers and staff
A staff-operated mentoring program to help students stay on track
Flexible scheduling
Opportunities for credit recovery
One-on-one and small group assistance available on Fridays 
Balanced course offerings - core and elective classes available 
A positive learning environment
A full Structured English Immersion (SEI) program 
A well-respected art program with opportunities to show and sell art in the community
A commitment to recognizing and awarding student success ​
