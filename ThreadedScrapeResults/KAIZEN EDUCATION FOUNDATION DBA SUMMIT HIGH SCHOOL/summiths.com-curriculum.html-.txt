Curriculum
Summit High School is dedicated to developing and implementing effective standards-aligned curriculum. Using Arizona's Academic Standards, teachers plan instruction that is relevant and rigorous to ensure students are gaining knowledge and valuable skills for their future lives.  Each course integrates reading, writing, and technology to maximize opportunities for students to refine and master skills in these areas. Textbooks and all other instructional materials are researched-based and support student proficiency of state standards.
