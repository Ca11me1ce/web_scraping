				OPEN Thursday - 2 Hour Delay			
						Please be advised that WPACES will be open on Thursday, March 22, 2018, with a 2-hour delay.					
WPACES will be open on Thursday, March 22, 2018, with a 2-hour delay.  Students should report to school no later than 10 AM.
Upper Darby District is closed tomorrow. Therefore, those students that ride the Upper Darby bus will not have transportation provided by them. The students who ride Southeast Delco and William Penn will have to listen to the news to see if they are also on a 2-hour delay or if those districts will be closed.
All students who ride Philly trans will be at their normal bus stop 2 hours later than usual tomorrow morning.
