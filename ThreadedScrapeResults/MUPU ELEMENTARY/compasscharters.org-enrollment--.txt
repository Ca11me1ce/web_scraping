Enrollment
Enrollment is as easy as 1-2-3. Select 
“Enroll Now”
 to begin.
We are excited you have chosen Compass Charter Schools as an educational option for your scholar. Our 2017 – 2018 school year is currently underway and enrollment is still open. Summer School enrollment begins 
March 1 and enrollment for the 2018-19 year also opens on 
March 1.
Compass Charter Schools is a free public charter school with year-round admissions for scholars in grades TK-12.

Compass Charter Schools is a network of California public schools. We admit all scholars who wish to attend. We are nonsectarian, do not discriminate, and do not charge tuition. The questions on our Online Application allow Compass Charter Schools to properly support each incoming scholar. The answers to these questions have no effect on admissions or enrollment decisions. By submitting the application, you are guaranteeing your child the opportunity to attend Compass Charter Schools.
CCS admits scholars and conducts all educational programs, activities, and employment practices without discrimination on the basis of sex, race, religion, national origin, ancestry, creed, sexual orientation, disability, or any other legally protected classification.
