For the 2018-2019 school year, the application period is January 9, 2018 to April 11, 2018.  All completed applications received by April 11, 2018 will be included in the April 16, 2018 public lottery. Applications received after 4:00 pm on April 11, 2018 will be added to a wait list in the order received. Accepted families are notified via email and they have one week to accept the space or the spot will be offered to the next applicant. Prior year applications do not roll over to the next school year. If you were on the wait list for 2017-2018, you must submit an application for 2018-2019.
Incoming siblings of returning students will be given priority as long as a 2018-2019 online application is submitted.
In addition to submitting an online application, parents must attend one mandatory New Parent Information Session. They are held at our campus: 10380 Spring Canyon Rd. 92131, in the multi-purpose room. You do not need to RSVP, but sign in upon your arrival. Children are welcome to attend, please bring materials to keep them occupied.
Frequently asked questions during the application period:
Q: What is the process for applying for the 2018-19 school year?
A: Starting January 9, 2018 and ending April 11, 2018, fill out an online application and attend an information session. All applications received by April 11, 2018, will be included in the April 16, 2018 lottery. Applications received after 4:00 pm April 11, 2018 will be added to a wait list in order received.
Q: Are siblings given priority?
A: Siblings of currently enrolled students are given priority. That said, an application for siblings is still required.
Q: I have a Kindergartner, how old must s/he be to enroll?
A: Kindergarten Applicants must be 5 years of age on or before September 1st for the 2018-19 school year.
Q: How do I apply?
A: Complete the online application.
