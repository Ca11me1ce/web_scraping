School Tours
Please take advantage of the many ways we share our school.
New Student Information Sessions
If you are enrolling for the first time at Innovations Academy you will be required to attend a New Parent Information Session.  Current information sessions have been scheduled for the following dates:
Saturday, June 10, 2017, 9:00 am
New Parent Information Sessions are held in the multi-purpose room on our campus. You do not need to RSVP, but sign in upon your arrival. Children are welcome to attend, please bring materials to keep them occupied. There is also a play structure outside the MPR that children are welcome to use.
Coffee Connections
Coffee Connections are a chance to sit and engage with our director, Christine Kuglen in topics of interest to the attendees. Come meet other parents and community members wishing to explore our. Coffee Connections take place on campus from 9:15-10:15 on the LAST Wednesday of the month.
Other Visits
If you are interested in visiting Innovations Academy, please give us a call ahead of time to schedule.
