	By Train from Manhattan (via A/C Train)
		Take the A Train towards Brooklyn to the Utica Avenue Station.
		Exit the train and walk in the direction of the train to the end of the station.
		Exit the station and walk down Fulton Street, crossing over Malcolm X Boulevard.
		One block down, make a left on Patchen Avenue.
		Excellence is five blocks down Patchen, between Macdonough and Macon Streets.
	By Train from Manhattan (via J Train)
		Take the J Train towards Queens to the Halsey Street Station.
		Walk up the block to Halsey Street (you will see a Dunkin' Donuts on the corner)
		Walk four blocks down Halsey Street in the direction of Saratoga Avenue.
		Make a left on Patchen Avenue.
		Excellence is one block down Patchen, between Macon and Macdonough Streets.
	By Car from Brooklyn Bridge
		Take the BQE to Exit 27, the Atlantic Avenue exit.
		Follow Atlantic Avenue past Atlantic Center and the Atlantic Rail Yards.
		Turn left on Utica Avenue under the elevated train tracks past the Atlantic Rail Yards (there is a small sign for the Utica Avenue exit under the elevated tracks).
		Follow Utica Avenue through Fulton Street (Utica Avenue will become Malcolm X Boulevard).
		Turn right on Macon Street.
		The school will be one block down Macon on Patchen Avenue. Parking is available on the street. Note: please look carefully at the street signs for "No Parking" day/time restrictions.
