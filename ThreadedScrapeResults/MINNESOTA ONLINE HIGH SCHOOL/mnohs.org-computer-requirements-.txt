				A Computer is Required			
	Print
Students are required to have the following to be successful at MNOHS:
1. Regular access to a relatively new computer with Windows 7 or higher or a Mac OS X version 10.9 or higher. 
2. High-speed internet access
3. A headset with microphone.
Accepted Computer Operating Systems: Windows 10, Windows 8, Windows 7 and MAC OS X 10.9 or higher.
Although some assignments and tasks can be accomplished on other types of devices, such as your smartphone, iPads, tablets, Kindles and Chromebooks, you will not be able to do most of your MNOHS course work on them.  You need regular access to a Windows or Mac computer to fully participate in your MNOHS classes.
