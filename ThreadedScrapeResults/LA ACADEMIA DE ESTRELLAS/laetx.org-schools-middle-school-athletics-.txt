Athletics
Mission Statement
Provide a culture that gives student-athletes an opportunity to develop success in academics and competition to achieve excellence in life.
Goals of LAE Athletes
Maintain an average of 70% higher in each class.
Be a teachable athlete that can be coached and trained by coaches.
Have a team player attitude on and off the field / court.
Compete for championships in our conference and state.
Goals of LAE Coaches
Lead, Mentor & Teach players in the areas of character and responsibility.
Create a disciplined, difficult and competitive environment.
Create a great relationship with the players.
