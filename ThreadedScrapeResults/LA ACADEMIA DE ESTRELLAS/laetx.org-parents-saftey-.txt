Campus Safety and Security
La Academia de Estrellas Charter School strives to maintain a safe and secure learning environment for our students and staff. In an effort to ensure the safety of the members of our learning community, the following procedures have been put in place:
Only LAE students will be allowed to enter the building during arrival through doors 5 and 6.
Parents, older siblings, former students or other visitors will not be allowed to enter the building during arrival or dismissal times.
Parents and other visitors wishing to enter the building at any time will be required to check-in through the front office beginning at 7:30 a.m. Picture ID is required.
Mesh or Clear backpacks will be required for 5th-8th grade students who bring a backpack for athletics/dance/karate/PE/cheerleading. (Effective Friday, 10/13/17).
In addition, parents are urged to monitor their children’s online activities closely to ensure their safety.
Parents should contact the school at one of the following numbers to report serious safety concerns:
682-325-9183 or 214-870-6509
