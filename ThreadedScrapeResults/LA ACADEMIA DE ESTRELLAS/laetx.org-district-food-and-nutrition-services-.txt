The Summer Food Service Program (SFSP) was created to ensure that children in low-income areas could continue to receive nutritious meals during long school vacations, when they do not have access to school lunch or breakfast. It provides nutritious and free meals to children 18 and younger during the summer months. This program is also available to disabled adults with proper ID. This 
pamphlet
 contains more information.
For more information on the Summer Food Service Program (SFSP) and to find a location near you please visit:
