Primary
4211 Watonga Blvd. Houston, TX 77092
	KIPP NEXUS is the newest primary school located on the Northwest side of Houston.  At NEXUS, joyful learning experiences are at the center of everything that we do through our school values of Nurture, Empower, Xcel, Unite, and Serve.  NEXUS students will receive a rigorous and balanced curriculum of math, reading, science, and social studies to build reasoning, analysis, and problem-solving skills.  Students will also have technology, music, and physical education daily to promote creativity and emotional balance.  Consisting of two gyms, a baseball field, football field, soccer field, and track, the NEXUS athletic facilities will provide a healthy, active way of life for students, families, and the community.  KIPP NEXUS is the central point for all KIPP Houston Public Schools athletic events.  We are committed to the academic and character development of every student for success in college and the competitive world beyond.  If you are looking for a school that helps students have pride and joy in who they are and what they are learning, look no further than KIPP NEXUS!

	KIPP Unity Primary is the newest KIPP primary school in Houston. At Unity, we believe that we are stronger when we work together. We take pride in our diverse community and embrace the uniqueness of each child. Unity focuses on the values of Love, Explore, Achieve, and Dream because we believe that character is just as important as academics. We want students to be leaders for transformative change and prepare our students for the future by encouraging them to be active global citizens.

6700 Bellaire Boulevard
Houston,TX 77074
	KIPP CONNECT Primary school is the elementary component of the new PreK through 12th-grade KIPP school that opened its doors in August 2014. Our primary school is modeled after the Purpose Built Communities Project in Atlanta. We hope to create not only a school for our students and families, but a hub that will be buzzing 24/7 with activities and resources to support and enrich the lives of our students’ families and the community. We strive to create a beautiful environment where our children are invited, motivated, and supported to explore learning, while falling in love with learning and with life.
