PARCC 2015 Results
Once again our Magónistas and teachers are demonstrating that their diligent work pays off! This year was one of glory for the classes of 2020 and 2024 as they showed the strongest gains across subject areas.
Some highlights from their work:
3rd Grade Reading/Writing performance was in the 73rd percentile.
3rd Grade Math performance was in the 65th percentile.
7th Grade Reading/Writing performance increased by 11 percentile points.
7th Grade Math performance increased by 20 percentile points.

CSAP Scores
Once again, RFMA students have posted outstanding results on the 2011 CSAP.  We are proud to say that our students percentages of Proficient or Advanced have exceeded both our district’s percentages for proficiency and the State of Colorado’s percentages for proficiency.
The class of 2020 scored:
91% proficient in Reading
59% proficient in Writing
100% proficient in Math
The class of 2019 scored:
77% proficient in Reading
82% proficient in Writing
100% proficient in Math
The class of 2018 scored:
79% proficient in Reading
92% proficient in Writing
88% proficient in Math
54% proficient in Science
For the 2009-2010 academic year, our students again succeeded in posting some of the highest CSAP scores in the state.
The class of 2022 (3rd Grade) scored:
95% proficient in Reading
60% proficient in Writing
95% proficient in Math
The class of 2021 (4th grade) scored:
84% proficient in Reading
58% proficient in Writing
an amazing 100% proficient in Math
According to the Colorado Department of Education, ethnically diverse and low-income students are falling behind on state standardized assessments and are failing to graduate.  The demographic population RFMA enrolled for the 2010-2011 academic year includes 93% of students qualifying for Free and Reduced lunch, 95% Latino and 86% of students classified as English Language Learners.

For the 2008-2009 academic year, the class of 2018 (3rd Grade) scored:
97% Proficient on the CSAP Reading
67% Proficient on CSAP Writing
100% Proficient on CSAP Math.
On average the percentage of our students who scored Proficient or Advanced is 47% higher on each assessment than the surrounding district.
