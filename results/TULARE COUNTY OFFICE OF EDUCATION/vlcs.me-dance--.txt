The Valley Life Charter School Dance Program incorporates:
Utilizing Dance Technique in various styles of dance. Understanding of Music and its inherent rhythm patterns. Learning the value of core fitness through the use of flexibility and strength exercises. Developing spatial awareness and memorization skills through learned choreography. Challenging students to stay focused and disciplined while physically moving the body. Using Leadership skills in the dance education process…And of course having a lot of fun! End Goal: A phenomenal opportunity for PERFORMANCE!!!
