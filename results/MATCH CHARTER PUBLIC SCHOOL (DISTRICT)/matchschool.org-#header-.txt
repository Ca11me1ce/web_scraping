Match is a free, high-performing charter public school in Boston
We prepare students in grades pre-K through 12 to succeed in college and beyond
During their careers at Match, students achieve and surpass grade-level standards, as measured by the most rigorous assessments in the nation. And they go on to enroll in and complete four-year colleges in high numbers.
