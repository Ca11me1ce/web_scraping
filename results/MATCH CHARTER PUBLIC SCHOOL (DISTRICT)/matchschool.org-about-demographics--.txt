Demographics
The demographics of Match students and Boston Public School (BPS) students are similar. Black and Hispanic students comprise 93% of Match's enrollment, as compared to 79% of BPS students. Match students also resemble BPS students on key measures of high need, including income status, English as a second language, and special education needs.
