Kelly Alfaro
 I Recruitment Manager
Ms. Alfaro is originally from New Hampshire. She has a Bachelor's degree from Saint Anselm College and a Master's degree from Boston University School of Education. She is passionate about serving students and families on their path to educational success and beyond. She is looking forward to finding the next generation of teachers for the Match community. In her free time she enjoys traveling, cooking, volunteering, and spending time with her family. Prior to joining Match, Ms. Alfaro served as the Director of Student Services for Breakthrough Greater Boston. She has also served in various educational roles at two separate charter schools focusing on family engagement, academic mentoring, and student services. Her career in education started with higher education recruitment at Saint Anselm College.

Jennifer Berylson Block
 | General Counsel
Ms. Block joined Match in March 2012 as the General Counsel.  Previously, she worked at the law firm of Goodwin Procter LLP from 2005-2012, as a member of the Private Equity group, primarily focusing on mergers and acquisitions, private equity transactions, corporate governance matters and the representation of early-and later-stage growth companies.  She also represented a large number of not-for-profit clients and played a large role in the firm's pro bono initiatives. Ms. Block received her B.A. from Williams College in 2001 and her J.D. from the University of Pennsylvania Law School in 2005.  She is admitted to practice in Massachusetts.

CHRIS CORREA
 | RECRUITMENT MANAGER
Mr. Correa began his career working with the Massachusetts Department of Youth Services, where he served as the Assistant Director of a program that focused on helping young men cope with substance abuse and addiction. Sheri Cheng 
I Director of Data Management and Analytics

EMILY STAINER
 I CHIEF ACADEMIC OFFICER
Ms. Stainer joined Match to support school leaders in delivering exceptional student outcomes. She came to Match Public Charter School to be part of building an exceptional pre-K to 12th grade experience for Boston students. Prior to that she worked at UP Education Network developing programming and instructional coaches for six turnaround schools in Massachusetts. Ms. Stainer previously served as the Head of School for Alma del Mar Charter School in New Bedford, MA. She started her education career at Citizen Schools in 2002 through the National Teaching Fellowship program and then served in variety of local, regional and national team roles. She has also served youths at City Year Columbia and the Swearer Center for Public Service at Brown University. She graduated from Brown University and later earned a Masters of Education from Lesley University.

 | Executive Director
