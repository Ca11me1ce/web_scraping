Enrollment
Thank you for your interest in Monument Academy. Monument Academy is a public school of choice. No tuition is charged with the exception of Preschool and full-day Kindergarten. Please keep in mind that the Colorado Department of Education guidelines and District 38 Policy states that a student must be 5 years of age by October 1st to enter Kindergarten and 6 years of age by October 1 to enter 1st grade. Please start the process by clicking on the Letter of Interest Form.
Letter of Interest Link
If you would like to check on the status of your submission, you may use the contact form at the bottom of this page. Due to limited class sizes, a seat must be offered prior to proceeding further into the registration process.
Forms are only accepted in completion,  no partial packets are accepted!

John Irwin School of Excellence Award Winner
The Colorado Department of Education has announced that for the 12th consecutive year Monument Academy has been named a John Irwin School of Excellence Award Winner.  
This prestigious award is given to the top 8% of Colorado Public Schools. Schools that demonstrate excellent academic achievement “exceeding” expectations on the state’s academic achievement indicator are recognized.
