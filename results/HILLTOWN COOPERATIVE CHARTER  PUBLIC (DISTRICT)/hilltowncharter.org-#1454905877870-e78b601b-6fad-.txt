OUR MISSION
Engaging students in hands-on interdisciplinary learning and the arts.
Creating an intimate community of students, staff, and families.
Cultivating children’s individual voices and respect for their world
Learn more
FEATURED STUDY
Reds & Oranges (Grades 4/5) Ancient China Study
From Oranges Teacher Kate Saccento: “
The Reds and Oranges recently completed a study on Ancient China.  During the unit, the students learned about the geography, language, dynasties, philosophies and religion of Ancient China. “

COMMUNITY HIGHLIGHT
Grandparents & Special Friends Day
May 4, 2018 ~10:00 a.m. – 12:15 p.m.
Friends of Hilltown welcomes grandparents and special adult friends to visit and experience the joy of learning at Hilltown Cooperative Charter Public School. Meet teachers and administrators, visit classrooms, view student artwork, and enjoy light refreshments. Registration begins at 10:00 a.m. Please RSVP by completing the registration form below by Monday, April 30, 2018.
You are also invited to stay for the All School Music Festival, an evening of musical performances by all Hilltown students starting at 6:30 p.m. at First Churches in Northampton.
