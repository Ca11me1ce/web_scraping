Announcements
SCMS opens its doors at 6:45 am daily. Students may eat breakfast then report to the gym.   First bell rings at 7:20 to notify students to go to their lockers to collect their books for morning classes.  1st hour bell rings at 7:30 a.m.  Students should be inside the classroom doors when the 7:30 am bell rings.
School dismisses at 2:40 pm.  Students must leave the building by 2:50 unless they are involved in an after school activity.
