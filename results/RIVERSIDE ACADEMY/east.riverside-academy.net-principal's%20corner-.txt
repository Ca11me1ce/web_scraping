Meet Principal Eman Radha
Eman Radha,
 Principal of Riverside Academy- East Elementary, has been a member of the Riverside-Academy administration since October 2005.  She holds a baccalaureate degree in Science, a Masters degree in Education Curriculum and Instruction from the University of Detroit Mercy, and a teaching certificate from the State of Michigan.      Over the last 18 years of her career in elementary education in the US, she worked with many different cultures in Metropolitan Detroit.  Principal Radha is currently working on an ESL endorsement at the U of M in Dearborn. This multicultural experience enriched her awareness and enhanced her skills as a school principal.
