Tony Aguirre Latino Men’s Basketball Tournament
February 17 @ 8:00 am - 
February 18 @ 5:00 pm
Event Navigation
« No School
President’s Day (No School) »
On President’s Day weekend the Guadalupe Centers, a non profit serving families since 1919 in KCMO, will host 15 basketball teams, their families and friends, from a seven state area at our Latino Men’s Basketball Tournament!. A full schedule of games will be played on Saturday February 17th and Sunday February 18th. This tournament is a non ticketed event, free and open to the public. Come cheer for your favorite team at a fun and safe environment. Awards ceremony will be held Sunday at the conclusion of play. Proceeds benefit the Guadalupe Centers Youth Programs.
Saturday starting at 6:00pm at the Guadalupe Centers located at 1015 Avenida Cesar E Chavez, KCMO, we will host a dance to welcome all of the teams
 with live entertainment featuring Max & Josh Baca of Los Texmaniacs & Steele Road Band, food and beverages sold (no coolers or bottles allowed). Tickets are $10 ($5 for players) Open to the public.
Tournaments will take place at 3 different locations (bracket available soon on our website):
Penn Valley Gym
3201 SW Trafficway KCMO
Guadalupe Centers High School (Alta Vista)
1524 Paseo Blvd. KCMO
Tony Aguirre Community Center
2050 W Penny St KCMO
