Workforce Development
The Guadalupe Centers aims to improve the quality of life for individuals that walk through our doors by assisting them in job placement, career development, educational and training opportunities and general workforce development knowledge. We have been working with the community and area employers for almost a century now and take pride in working with you to help better your life and our community.
The Workforce Development program looks to offer clients the opportunity to develop job readiness and life/family skills. These opportunities are offered in the way of:
Literacy classes
Basic computer classes
Certification programs (such as the culinary program)
ESL classes
Onsite employer interviews
Family reading program
Other
If you are interested in enhancing your personal and professional career please contact us to learn more about programs and classes currently being offered.
