Student Application Form
The application for the 2018-2019 school year is now open!! Thank you for your patience and understanding.
To complete your application, click on this link: 
New Student Application Form
To apply for the 2017-2018 school year, please email: info@innovationsacademy.org to inquire about space availability. 
All completed applications received by April 11, 2018 will be included in the April 16, 2018 public lottery. Applications received after 4:00 pm on April 11, 2018 will be added to a wait list in the order received. Accepted families are notified via email and they have one week to accept the space or the spot will be offered to the next applicant.
The lottery will be held on Monday, April 16, 2018 at 1:00 pm. First round acceptance letters will be sent via email on April 18, 2018. Wait listed students will be notified via email by April 23, 2018. If you do not hear from us by these dates, you are welcome to email Tami at info@innovationsacademy.org
Submission of an online application does not guarantee placement at Innovations Academy. One application per student (with the exception of twins or triplets). Attendance at a New Parent Information Session is not required before submitting an application; however, it is mandatory prior to your child attending IA.
