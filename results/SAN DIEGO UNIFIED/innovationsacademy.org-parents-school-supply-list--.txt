School Supply List 17-18
As a public charter school, Innovations Academy will provide the school supplies required for the school year. If, however, you would like to support the classroom and the school by donating materials, here is a list of supplies that we will need in our classrooms. Please have these supplies by the first day of school.
Parents of kindergarten students, please see the kindergarten class supply list posted 
here
.
The below supply list is for basic materials. Individual teachers will make specific requests as needed for their classrooms by the end of the first week of school.
3- composition books
4- spiral bound notebooks (college or wide ruled)
1 box of 24 colored pencils
1 box of 24 colored crayons (for grades K-5)
1 box of 12 colored felt tip markers (thin)
1-backpack, LABELED with child’s name
1-lunch bag or box, LABELED with child’s name
1 reusable water bottle, LABELED with child’s name
1 box tissues
4-6 whiteboard markers
erasers
4- two pocket folders (different colors)
3 glue sticks
1 small bottle of white glue
1 roll of blue painters tape (if your child’s last name starts with A-L)
1 box of multi-sized band aids  (if your child’s last name starts with M-Z)
liquid soap for hand washing (if your child’s last name starts with A-L)
hand sanitizer (if your child’s last name starts with M-Z)
3×5 lined index cards
1 ream white copy paper
1 pack of sheet protectors
