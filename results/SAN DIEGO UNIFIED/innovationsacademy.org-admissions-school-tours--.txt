Getting to Know Innovations Academy
Please take advantage of the many ways we share our school.
New Parent Information Sessions
If you are enrolling for the first time at Innovations Academy you will be required to attend a New Parent Information Session. All information sessions are held in the multi-purpose room on our campus. Current information sessions have been scheduled for the following dates:
Tour and Get Information…The only way to make an informed choice for your children is to get accurate information. Come find out about our school. Parent information sessions are the way to learn about our program and tour our school.
Wednesday, November 29th at 6:00 p.m.
 Wednesday, December 6th at 6:00 p.m.
 Saturday, January 6th at 9:00 a.m.
Wednesday, February 21st at 6:00 p.m.
Saturday, March 10th at 9:00 a.m.
Wednesday, April 11th at 6:00 p.m.
Saturday, April 28th at 9:00 a.m.
You do not need to RSVP, but sign in upon your arrival. Children are welcome to attend, please bring materials to keep them occupied. There is also a play structure outside the MPR that children are welcome to use.
Coffee Connections
Coffee Connections are a chance to sit and engage with our director, Christine Kuglen in topics of interest to the attendees. Come meet other parents and community members wishing to explore our. Coffee Connections take place on campus from 9:15-10:15 on the LAST Wednesday of the month.
Exhibition Nights
Families are welcome to visit the classrooms during our Exhibition Nights on Thursday, December 14, 2017 and Thursday, March 22, 2018 from 5:00-7:30 pm. Please remember that Exhibition Nights are a showcase for our students to their families, and we ask you to use this time for observing and save any teacher questions you may have for a later date.
