Lottery Information
 2018-2019 School Year Lottery Procedure
The 2018-2019 public lottery will be held on Monday, April 16, 2018 at 1:00 pm. First round acceptance notices will be emailed on Wednesday, April 18, 2018.
Applications received after 4:00 pm on April 11, 2018 will be added to a wait list in the order received. As space becomes available, applicants from the wait list are notified via mail.
The Open Application Period for the 2018-2019 school year is from January 9, 2018 through April 11, 2018.  Prior year applications do not roll over to the next school year.  Incoming siblings of returning students will be given priority as long as a 2018-2019 online application is submitted.
