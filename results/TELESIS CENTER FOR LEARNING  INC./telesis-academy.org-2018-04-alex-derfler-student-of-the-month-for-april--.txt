Alex Derfler – Student of the Month for April!
Alex Derfler is a Junior Academy-7 student. His favorite classes are science and math and his favorite teacher is Miss Poppleton.  Alex likes electronics, games, and SpaceX.  He is proud that he has built two computers and placed second in a STEMASU challenge. 
His teacher’s said, “Alex has overcome obstacles and continues to thrive and grow.  He puts forth 110% effort in all work, frequently going above and beyond. He’s creative and has a great sense of humor. “
His math teacher also complimented him on turning in the most amazing math project!
Fantastic job, Alex!
