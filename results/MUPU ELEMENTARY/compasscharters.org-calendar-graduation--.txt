Graduation
Graduation
Congratulations to the Class of 2017! We were so honored to celebrate the graduation of our High School seniors this month. Graduation ceremonies began in Sonoma, then moved on to Fresno. We concluded with graduation ceremonies in Los Angeles and finally, San Diego. We rejoiced, laughed and cried with these amazing scholars. We cannot wait to see where these innovative, creative, self-directed scholars go as they navigate their future. Keep in touch graduates! We are so proud of you all!
