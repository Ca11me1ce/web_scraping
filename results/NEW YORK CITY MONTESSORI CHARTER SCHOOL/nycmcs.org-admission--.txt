Application period is now open for the 2018/2019 School Year.
 The application period is January 2rd 2018 through Friday, March 30, 2018 @ 6pm. 
Our lottery will be held on Friday April 13th @ 10am. 
Applicants can fill out the application below or at our offices located at:
423 East 138th street, Bronx NY 10454. 
Please call 347.226.9094 with any questions. 
The NYCMCS Lottery for the 2018/2019 School Year will be held on Friday, April 13th at 10AM. 
The lottery will be held at the school’s location: 
423 East 138th Street
Bronx NY 10454
Attendance is not mandatory. Parents will be notified of their child’s placement via mail.
