Employment Office
Click Here for Current Job Opportunities
The employment office is the point of contact for applicants and departments regarding                                 the hiring process. The employment office is responsible for posting all position                                 vacancy announcements weekly, coordinating all position advertising, maintaining current                                 job openings, and assisting departments with recruiting. The employment office also                                 maintains all employee personnel files, processes employment status change forms,                                 performs HRIS data entry and maintains employee data integrity, and coordinates New                                 Employee Orientation.
