Board of Trustees
A Board of Trustees governs the Global Learning Charter Public School. The Board of Trustees is comprised of local leaders who have expertise in education, economics, business, and other areas. The Board of Trustees meets as a whole once a month to set policy and provide oversight to the school. Breakout committees also meet on a monthly basis to focus on a primary effort. These committees include: the development committee; an education committee; the finance committee; and, the governance committee.
