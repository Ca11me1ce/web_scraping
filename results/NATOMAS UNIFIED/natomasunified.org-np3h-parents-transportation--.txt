Transportation
NP3 High School provides bus transportation throughout Natomas, including north and south Natomas and Regency Park. Buses run before and after school :
   Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday buses depart at 3:15 pm.
   Early Dismissal Thursdays and Minimum Days buses leave at 1:15 pm.
  There is an additional bus that leaves the campus daily (Mon. – Fri.) at 4:15 pm  (for students who stay for school activities  or receive help with homework)
Bus Service For 2016 – 2017
The Natomas Unified School District (NUSD) Transportation Department is processing bus pass applications electronically. Parents can now begin submitting applications for the 2016-2017 school year.
The bus pass application link can be found at: 
 The District Transportation Department.
 You may also check the “Bus Pass Application” button on the right side of our home page or go to the District’s website. (Look for Transportation Department’s web page. ) When available, your child’s school pictures will be printed on their bus identification cards. If a school picture in not on file, a NUSD transportation staff member will contact you so that you can schedule an appointment to bring your child in to the District office to have their picture taken. Students will be assigned to the safest established school bus stop that is closest to their homes.
Our staff is looking forward to working together with you and your children to provide safe and dependable bus transportation during the 2016 – 2017 school year. If you have any questions regarding this process or need assistance submitting your application, please contact the Transportation Department at (916) 567-5493.
