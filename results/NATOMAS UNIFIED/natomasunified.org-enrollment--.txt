Transfers From One NUSD School to Another NUSD School
Intradistrict School Transfers – Voluntary (ISTV) request should be completed if you live in the boundaries of one NUSD school but want to request to attend a different NUSD school
If the student is 
not currently enrolled in an NUSD school, please complete the online registration packet by clicking on the “Register Now” star above
Students who are currently attending their requested school do not need to resubmit a transfer request for subsequent years
ISTVs are accepted for all enrollment periods and are approved based on space availability at the requested school, among other factors
Submit a transfer request online in English
Presentar una solicitud de transferencia en línea en español
Student Enrollment Transfers Between NUSD and Different Districts
Interdistrict Transfer Permits (ITP) allow students to attend schools outside the Natomas Unified School District where they are not residents. If you are interested in more information regarding the ITP process, please call School Leadership and Support at 916-567-5424 or click on buttons below.
