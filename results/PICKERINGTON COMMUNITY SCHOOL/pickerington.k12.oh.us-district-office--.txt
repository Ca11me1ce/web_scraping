District Office Information
Hours
Standard hours for the District Office are 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. weekdays.
Summer office hours for all Pickerington Schools buildings will be 7:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. weekdays.
We will resume standard office hours in August.
Location
Pickerington Schools District Office is located at 90 N. East Street, Pickerington, Ohio, adjacent to Heritage Elementary School.
