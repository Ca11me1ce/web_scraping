100%
Commencement 
2018
The Ninth Annual Commencement Exercises of Urban Prep Academies will be held on Saturday, June 16, 2018 at the Civic Opera House located at 20 N. Upper Wacker Drive, Chicago, IL 60606. 230 young men will walk across the stage to receive their high school diplomas during the event.  The graduating seniors—all African-American males—hail from Urban Prep's Englewood, West and Bronzeville campuses.  For the ninth consecutive year, 100% of Urban Prep’s graduating class were accepted to four-year colleges and universities (for more information about this achievement, please 
click here
).
 To RSVP for this year's Commencement Exercises, please visit
