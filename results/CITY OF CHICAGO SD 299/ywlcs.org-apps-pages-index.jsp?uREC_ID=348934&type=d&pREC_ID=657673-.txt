Young Women’s Mentoring Program
YW Mentor Program was created to provide additional, individualized support for our
students’ personal, academic, and professional growth and development, ultimately
culminating in college readiness, college success, and career preparation.
What’s Required:
• Participate in the mentor program at least one school year .
• Participate in site-based mentor-mentee activities at YWLCS (as applicable to your mentor-mentee relationship)
• Weekly mentor-mentee communications (phone, email, or text) (preferred) (All email communications should be copied to ywlcsmentors@ywlcs.org)
• Allow for open communication between mentor and family of mentee
• Communicate with the ywlcsmentors@ywlcs.org email if any challenges with mentees arise
• Attend the Meet & Greet for Mentors, Mentees, and Mentees’ families.
• Complete a required Department of Children and Family Services background check and federal background check.
