ComEd Icebox Derby - Kiarra Pearson
ComEd Icebox Derby - Kiarra Pearson 
Kiarra Pearson was selected based on her application. 30 girls were chosen to compete. 
The purpose of the 2017 ComEd Icebox Derby STEM cup challenge is to continue to encourage young girls to lean into the role that innovations such as solar and technology can play throughout the derby events in order to further enhance STEM learning opportunities. 
The teams that cross the finish line in the shortest total time based on completing the preliminary race heat as well as the correct completion of the STEM challenges.
There are 30 participant prizes available to be won. Each participant prize includes $1,000 awarded in the form of a check.
One final grand prize championship three-lap race will be conducted between the vehicles of the two teams that win a preliminary race heat.
There are 5 grand prizes available to be won. Each grand prize includes one (1) 13- inch laptop/notebook and $1,500 awarded to the winner in the form of a check.
