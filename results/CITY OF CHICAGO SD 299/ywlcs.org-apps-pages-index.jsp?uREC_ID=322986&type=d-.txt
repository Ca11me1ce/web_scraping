												Apply Now!									
By law, charter schools are open to all students and cannot charge tuition or administer admission tests. If you are a young woman entering grades 9-12 and live in the city of Chicago, you are eligible to attend YWLCS.
To becoming a YWLCS student in the 2018-2019 school year:
YWCLS will participate in the GoCPS common application system.  Applicants can create an account by visiting http://go.cps.edu/.  YWLCS is listed as YOUNG WOMENS HS.  Account information can be obtained from your current elementary school.  Apply using the go.cps.edu website and select YOUNG WOMENS HS.  
Open House/Shadow Days:
To attend an open house or schedule a shadow day contact contact Mrs. Renee M. Sherrod at rsherrod@ywlcs.org or (312) 949-9400 ext. 123.​​​​​​​
Transfer Students (10th and 11th Grade Only)
1.  Complete the 
online
​​​​​​​ application for the 2017-2018 School Year. Alternatively, please click here for a hard copy that can be turned into the YWLCS main office.
2.  Request official transcript from current school and submit to YWLCS, Attn:  Ms. Danielle Nightingale, dnightingale@ywlcs.org or 949-9142 (fax).  Transcripts must be reviewed prior to acceptance to determine grade level placement.
3.  After the transcript review the applicant will be notified about their admissions status.  
If you have any questions or concerns about the application process contact our main office at 312-949-9400.
