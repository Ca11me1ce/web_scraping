												Alumnae 									
Since opening our doors in the fall of 2000, YWLCS has graduated more than 450 students. Students from YWLCS have gone on to such collegiate institutions as Northwestern University, Oberlin College, Pomona College, Smith College, and Agnes Scott College. To see a complete listing, 
click here
.   
Alumnae Statistics
Approximately 55% of YWLCS alumnae have graduated from college or are working to earn a degree. By comparison, only 8% of all Chicago public school graduates will earn a college degree by the age of 25. 
100% of 2015 graduates were accepted to college.
Transcript Request Policy - Requesting your High School Transcript
Ask for your transcript well in advance.
Complete the transcript request form below and submit to the main office in person, by fax at (312) 949-9142, or mail with payment (see below for payment details). Our mailing address is as follows: YWLCS, 2641 S. Calumet Ave., Chicago, IL 60616.
Please Note:
Immediate transcripts are available the same day at a fee of $5.00. Processing time may require 1-4 hours as determined by the availability of the College Prep Office; the College Prep Office is open Monday-Friday from 7:30am-3:30pm. 
Regular transcripts are available 5-7 business days after the transcript form is received at a cost of $3.00. 
Payments must be in the form of cash or money order. NO PERSONAL CHECKS will be accepted.
