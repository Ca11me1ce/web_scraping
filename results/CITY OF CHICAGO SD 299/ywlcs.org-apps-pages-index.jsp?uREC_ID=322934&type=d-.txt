The YWLCS Board of Directors is an active governing board. A maximum of 50 civic, business and not-for-profit leaders are elected to serve three-year terms on the YWLCS board. For a listing of our current board officers and members, please scroll down. To learn more about our Associate Board, please 
click here
.
The Board has the following three core fiduciary responsibilities:
To establish long-term strategic direction for the school through a regularly updated strategic plan. 
To provide oversight of the implementation of the strategic plan, the school’s programs, and the school’s leadership. 
To ensure adequate financial resources to realize the mission of YWLCS.
