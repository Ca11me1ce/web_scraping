Careers
Currently seeking:
 Teachers
Must be engaging, energetic and creative. We are seeking someone who is passionate about children and learning. 
REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION TO BE PRESENTED AND/OR BEFORE OF SIGNING CONTRACT WITH AEE:
EMPLOYMENT APPLICATION (filled in all its areas) (updated residential/mailing address and phone numbers).
Updated Resume
Copy of College Transcripts (if applicable)
Copy of Teaching Certification(s)
Background Check (FBI AND BCI)-- no person can start working until background check is completed
Copy of Social Security Card
Copy of Birth Certificate
Completed W-2 Form
Completed Payroll Forms
Copy of Voided Personal Check Account (for Direct Deposit purposes)
Anti-Fraud Statement (signed)
STRS/SERS documentation
