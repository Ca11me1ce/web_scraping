2018-19 Student Applications
2018-19 applications became available on February 1, 2018.  All applications received before February 1st will only go onto the wait lists for the current (2017-18) school year and do NOT carry over to 2018-19. 
Paper applications for 2018-19 are available at both the Kiest and Hampton locations and the 2018-19 online application is available 
here
.
