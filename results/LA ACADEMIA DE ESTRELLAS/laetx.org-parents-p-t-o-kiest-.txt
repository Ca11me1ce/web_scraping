The LAE PTO meets regularly during the school year at our Kiest location.  The PTO includes parents and staff who want to improve learning for all students.  Our PTO works together with the school staff to sponsor numerous schoolwide events, including Family Fun Nights, Daddy/Daughter & Mommy/Son Dances and Student Performances.  In addition, the PTO sponsors various service projects, including Thanksgiving baskets and holiday gift assistance for families in need.
