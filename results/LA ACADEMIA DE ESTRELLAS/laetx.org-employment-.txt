Thank you for your interest in La Academia de Estrellas Charter School!
We are always searching for the most talented and highly qualified individuals to fill our ranks.
All teacher applicants must either be SBEC-certified or in an alternative certification program in order to be considered for employment.  We prefer that applicants have at least two years of teaching experience.
We have currently have these open positions for 2018-19 (appropriate certifications needed for teaching positions):
Elementary Music Teacher
Middle School ELA Teacher
Middle School Science Teacher
