Salary and Budget Transparency Report
Transparency Reporting - Section 18(2) of the Public Act 94 of 1979, the State School Aid Act, has been amended and now requires each school district and intermediate school district to post certain information on its website.  This Budget and Transparency Report is an opportunity to communicate with our community on how we use the resources that are provided to Relevant Academy of Eaton County  
The Relevant Academy budget is adopted by our Board of Education no later than June 30 of each year.  This budget is posted on the website within 15 days of adoption.  Any subsequent budget amendments are also posted with 15 days of adoption.
