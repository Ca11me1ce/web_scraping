Michigan Merit Curriculum - Courses Provided
The courses that Relevant Academy learners need to earn credit toward their diploma is provided through Compass Learning.   In addition, our learners have regular communication with the Learning Coaches who provide the support they need to navigate through their courses.
Access to Current Technology
Relevant Academy learners can earn iPads to have access to their online coursework and to be able to check in daily with their Learning Coach.
