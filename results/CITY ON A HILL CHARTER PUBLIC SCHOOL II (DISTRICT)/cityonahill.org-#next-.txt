City on a Hill Circuit Street
CoaH 
Circuit Street
The home of City on a Hill's original charter, CoaH Circuit Street is located just a short walk from Dudley Square. This new building opened in 2007, and in 2017 Circuit Street graduated its 20th class. CoaH Circuit Street offers a unique college prep and civics based curriculum to prepare students to succeed in college.
CoaH Circuit Street accepts students entering the 9th grade only, by random lottery.

City on a Hill Dudley Square
CoaH 
Dudley Square
City on a Hill Dudley Square is our second location and opened in 2013. Located in the heart of Dudley Square, they graduated their first senior class in June 2017. CoaH Dudley Square offers students a college prep and civic focused curriculum to prepare them for success in college.
CoaH Dudley Square accepts students entering the 9th grade only, by random lottery.

City on a Hill New Bedford
CoaH 
New Bedford
City on a Hill's newest location, CoaH New Bedford opened in 2014 and will graduate its first senior class in 2018. Located in the heart of the historic, cobblestoned downtown of New Bedford, CoaH NB offers a unique college prep and civic focused curriculum to prepare students to succeed in college.
CoaH New Bedford accepts students entering the 9th grade only, by random lottery.
