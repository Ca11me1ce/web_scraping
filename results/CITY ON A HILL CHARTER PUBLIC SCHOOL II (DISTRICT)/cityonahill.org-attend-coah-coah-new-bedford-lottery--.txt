CoaH in New Bedford
City on a Hill in New Bedford
2018-2019 School Year
City on a Hill only admits students who are entering grade 9. 
City on a Hill is open to all students residing in Massachusetts. Preference in the lottery is given to siblings of current students and residents of New Bedford.
2018-19 School Year:
The main lottery for the 2018-19 school year took place on March 14th, 2018.
There will be another lottery on May 14th, 2018.  Enter a lottery
 form
 by May 13, 2018 at 11:59 PM to be included.
