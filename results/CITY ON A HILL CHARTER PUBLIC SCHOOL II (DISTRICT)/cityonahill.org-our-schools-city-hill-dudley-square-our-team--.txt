Our Team
You can reach any member of CoaH Dudley Square’s team by dialing the school’s main number, (617) 516-5888, and entering their extension.
Leadership
Sonya Pratt
 /  Principal / ext. 9403
B.A. Salem State College,  M.Ed. Northeastern University
Sonya Pratt has worked for the City on a Hill organization since 2002.  Ms. Pratt began at City on a Hill as a teaching fellow in the History department.  She later served as the Dean of Citizenship and Vice Principal of the City on a Hill flagship school in Roxbury before becoming principal of City on a Hill Dudley Square.  As a member of the City on a Hill organization Ms. Pratt helped found the Freshman Academy program, which is a staple program, integral to the City on a Hill culture.  A life-long resident of Boston, Ms. Pratt received her Bachelor’s degree in Criminal Justice from Salem State College and a Master’s of Education from Northeastern University. 
Laura Edouard
 /  Vice Principal & History Teacher  / ext. 9404
B.A. University of Rochester,  M.A.T. Simmons College
Laura Tobias Edouard is originally from Dorchester Massachusetts and grew up going to Boston Public Schools. She left home to attend the University of Rochester in Rochester, NY where she double majored in Anthropology and African & African American Studies. After college, she lived and taught in Namibia, Southern Africa for two years. Those two years solidified her love for teaching and when she came home, she applied to the City on a Hill Fellowship Program. She has been with City on a Hill Public Charter Schools since 2007 serving as a History Teacher, a Dean of Citizenship and now the Vice Principal of City on a Hill Dudley Square.
