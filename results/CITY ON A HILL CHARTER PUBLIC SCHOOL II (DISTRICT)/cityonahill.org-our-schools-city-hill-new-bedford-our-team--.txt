Our Team
You can reach any member of the CoaH New Bedford team by dialing the school’s main number, 508-985-6400 and entering their extension.
Leadership
Gail Keith
 /  Principal / 508-985-6402
B.S. Georgia Institute of Technology, M.Ed. University of Maine
Gail Keith joined City on a Hill New Bedford in July of 2017.  Gail began her post-secondary education at Georgia Tech – Goooooo Jackets! – and graduated with a BS in Industrial Design.  After college, she moved to Maine where she began her educational career and received her Masters in Education Leadership from the University of Maine.  Gail started as a classroom teacher, and over time served as a technology director, curriculum director, principal, and assessment coordinator before moving to Rhode Island in 2011.  After settling in Portsmouth, RI, Gail worked as a consultant with the Rhode Island Department of Education for two years before coming to work in New Bedford in July of 2014 as the Chief of Teaching and Learning at New Bedford High School.  Gail fell in love with the city and its students and feels blessed to continue her work in that community.
Amanda Kolby
 / Vice Principal of Instruction / 508-985-6415
B.S Daeman College, M.A University of Phoenix
This is Amanda Kolby’s first year at City on a Hill New Bedford. She began supporting students and families at New Bedford High School in 2015.  Amanda moved to Massachusetts after beginning her career in Colorado.  Her background is in Special Education and she has had the pleasure to work with with students with mild/moderate to severe/profound needs in K-12+ settings.  Amanda earned her BS from Daemen College, her MA at the University of Phoenix and completed her Certificate of Advanced Graduate Studies at the University of Denver.
