Our Students
The Circuit Street student body is made up of a variety of ages, ethnicities, learning levels and populations. We highly value our school’s diversity.
Accomplishments of Circuit City Graduates:
2016 & 2015 Valedictorians featured in the 
Boston Globe
85% of the class of 2016 were accepted into a 4 year college
81% of the class of 2016 received merit based scholarships
Over 5400 hours of community service completed by the class of 2016
