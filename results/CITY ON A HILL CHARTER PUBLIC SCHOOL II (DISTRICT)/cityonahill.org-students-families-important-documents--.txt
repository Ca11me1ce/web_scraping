Incident Report Form
If you are made aware of any threatening behavior or bullying incidents in school, on school grounds, or during school related activities, please report it and either:
Fill out an 
Incident Report form
,
Email 
incidentreport@cityonahill.org
, or
Anonymously call the Incident report line at 617-445-1515 ext. 532
Please note: You do not need to leave your name or contact information for your report to be investigated, however, no disciplinary action will be taken based solely on an anonymous report.
