Board of Directors
Michael Grossman, President
Michael is an equity research analyst at MFS Investment Management.
Steve LaBrie, Clerk
Steve has over 10 years of experience in equity sales and currently works at Deutsche Bank in Institutional Equity Sales.
Sally Bachofer
Sally is Executive Director of City on a Hill Charter Public Schools.
Dan Cavanaugh
Dan is a Senior Vice President at Jones Lang LaSalle. On the Downtown Leasing group, Mr. Cavanaugh focuses his efforts on representing both tenants, landlords and investors in the Boston Office market.
Maile Clark
Maile has over 20 years experience as a buy side equity research analyst.
Tripp Jones
Tripp is a principal at 21c, providing strategy and advisory services to businesses (for-profit and nonprofit) and investors looking to scale especially promising social enterprises that can substantially impact the sectors in which they operate.
