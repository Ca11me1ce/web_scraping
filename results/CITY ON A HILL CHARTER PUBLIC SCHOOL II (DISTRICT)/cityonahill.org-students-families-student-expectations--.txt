Student Expectations
City on a Hill is a safe, small, and serious school. All students are expected to maintain this culture by respecting their peers, their learning space, City on a Hill faculty and staff, and themselves.
Please be sure to read and become familiar with the City on a Hill 
Student Handbook
.
At City on a Hill, students are expected to:
Be on time every day
Be prepared to learn
Have all necessary classroom materials (books, pens, etc.) before class begins
Complete all homework
Take responsibility for and accept the consequences of their actions
In turn, students can expect from City on a Hill:
A safe and supportive environment to learn
Caring faculty to go to for guidance and support
A rigorous curriculum to prepare for college
Individualized support to reach academic goals and create a post graduation plan that best meets each student’s needs.
City on a Hill does not tolerate:
Derogatory language
Uniform violations – Please view the 
Uniform Policy Handbook
Threatening language or behavior
Student insubordination
Any form of bullying, cyber bullying, or retaliation – Please view the bullying prevention and intervention 
plan
