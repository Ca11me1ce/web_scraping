Do you need a copy of your high school transcript?
If your high school is currently operational please contact the campus from which you graduated during school hours. You can find school contact information by 
clicking here
.
If the school from which you graduated is now closed, please call the front desk at Responsive Education Solutions to request the transcript. 972-316-3663
Have the following information ready at the time of your request:
Student’s Full Name (include maiden name, if applicable)
Student’s Date of Birth
Student’s Social Security Number
Campus Student Attended – this must include the city, not just “Premier High School”
Year graduated/attended
Contact information to include: Phone, E-mail, Shipping Address
If you have any questions please email 
alumni@responsiveed.com
Responsive Education Solutions 
1301 Waters Ridge Dr.
Lewisville, TX 75057
Phone: 972.316.3663
Fax: 972.315.9506
