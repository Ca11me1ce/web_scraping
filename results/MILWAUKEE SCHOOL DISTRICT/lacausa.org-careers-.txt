Thank you for your interest in La Causa, Inc., where the best people work. In fact, nearly 400 employees in six divisions and six locations have been making a difference for over 40 years! Together we work to provide families with quality, comprehensive services so they can enjoy a healthy lifestyle. We hope you’ll join us!
La Causa, Inc. is an AA/EEOC/LEP Employer and Service Provider and participates in E-Verify. EOE of Minorities/Females/Vets/Disability.
