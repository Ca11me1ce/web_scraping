Boucher Automotive Group, Inc. Generously Donates Holiday Gifts for Kids    
    January 2, 2018  
News
 Posted in             
Social Services Youth Programs
The Boucher Automotive Group, Inc. made the holidays truly special for over 300 children and their families at La Causa Social Services Youth Program's Annual Holiday Party.  The Boucher Group's generosity was far-reaching.  The evening started with a wonderful, holiday meal that everyone enjoyed, followed by the company's generous donation of  over 300 gifts for the kids - from  basketballs, dolls, soccer balls, costume jewelry, toys, warm apparel, to Amazon Fire, and more -  that will be enjoyed for a long, long, time. We want to thank the Boucher Automotive Group. Inc. for their continued support and generosity.
