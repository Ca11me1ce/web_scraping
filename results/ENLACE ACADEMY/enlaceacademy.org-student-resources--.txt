Lexia Core 5
Lexia is a Pre-K-5 literacy tool that is built around the specific needs of each student.  The program is designed to adapt based on students responses and provide real-time personalization while students work independently online.  Whether in the classroom or at home, real-time data from student interaction is sent to the teacher, fueling customized lesson plans based on each student's needs.
To access, students need their Enlace Academy teacher's email address which can be found on our staff directory.

ST Math
Spatial-Temporal (ST) Math® is the leader in visual math instruction.  ST Math is game-based instructional software for K-12 and is designed to boost math comprehension and proficiency through visual learning.
