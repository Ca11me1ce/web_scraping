Regional Occupational Program (ROP)
Our students have enrolled in ROP through Vantage Point to earn credits in careers such as Dental, Automotive Technology, Veterinary Science, Child Development, Fire Science, and Sports Medicine.  Get the training you need for the job you want" is the motto of the 49er Regional Occupational Program (49er R.O.P.). 49er R.O.P. is a public education program offering career preparation classes to over 1,800 local high school students and adults each year.
