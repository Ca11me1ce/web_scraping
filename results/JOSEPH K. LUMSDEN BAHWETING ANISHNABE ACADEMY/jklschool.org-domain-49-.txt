Mrs. Laurie Jarvie
Mrs. Jarvie began working at JKL in 2006 after graduating from Ferris State University in 2005 with a degree in elementary education.  She also earned a master's degree in curriculum, instruction, and assessment from Walden University. This will be her twelfth year at JKL teaching second grade.  Mrs. Jarvie resides in Rudyard with her husband, Matt.  She has two grown children, Erin and Graham.  Mrs. Jarvie loves animals and has one dog, five cats, a horse, and a cow.  Her hobbies inlcude being outdoors, camping, and being together with family.
