Committees
Committees have been set up by the Board of Trustees to provide the support needed to achieve the FCCS mission.  Committee membership is open to parents, teachers, and community members.  Please consult the 
school calendar
 for committee meeting times. 
Academics Committee
This committee is responsible for making recommendation to the Board in terms of the school's academic program and implementation, and working with staff to help implement high academic standards.
Co-Chairs: Jodi Popielaski and Justine Somani, 
academics@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Nkechi Ileka-Adeoye, 
nkechi.ileka@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Admissions Committee
This committee works closely with the Head of School and FCPS personnel to facilitate the annual lottery and enrollment of new students throughout the year.
Chair:  Laura Mears, 
admissions@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Jeremy Hurley, 
jeremy.hurley@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Advocacy Committee
This committee is responsible for improving the operating environment for charter schools by ensuring that they have fair funding and the freedom to innovate.  The committee will focus on improving state and local policy and law and educating parents about school choice.
Chair:  Vacant, Acting Chair-Anne Sechler, 
advocacy@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Allison Rizzo, 
allison.rizzo@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Communications Committee
This committee is responsible for improving the communications of the school and also for community outreach efforts.
Chair:  Acting Chair-Jerryl Somani, 
communications@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Allison Rizzo, 
allison.rizzo@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Facilities Committee
This committee is responsible for ensuring that our facility is well-maintained and meets the needs of the academic and extra-curricular programs.
Chair: Vacant, 
facilities@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Phil Anderson, 
phil.anderson@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Finance Committee
This committee is responsible for ensuring that our budget is prepared and implemented in a fiscally conservative manner.
Chair:  Vacant, 
finance@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Jen Schneider, 
jen.schneider@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Fundraising Committee
This committee is responsible for planning and executing fundraising activities.
Chair:  Robyn Maslan, 
fundraising@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Jen Schneider, 
jen.schneider@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Grants Committee
This committee is responsible for identifying and pursuing opportunities for grants.
Chair:  Sarah Gemmell, 
grants@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Jeremy Hurley,  
jeremy.hurley@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Social Committee
This committee is responsible for planning and implementing social events throughout the year.
Chair:  Nanci Jacobo, 
social@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Shelly Smallwood, 
shelly.smallwood@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Library Committee
This committee is responsible for acquiring and sorting books, and running book fairs.
Chair:  Martina Hatley, 
library@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Jen Schneider, 
jen.schneider@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Volunteer Committee
This committee is responsible for organizing volunteers, especially for onsite volunteer work, such as classroom assistance, lunchroom duty, and administrative assistance.  Current volunteer needs are listed on this website, please 
click here
.
Chair:  Lauren Drennan, 
volunteer@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Board Liaison: Shelly Smallwood, 
shelly.smallwood@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
Yearbook Committee
This committee is responsible for publishing the annual yearbook, including managing the photography, writing, and financing of this effort.
Chair:  Tara Dodson, 
yearbook@frederickclassicalcharterschool.org
