Super Sitter Babysitting Class- June 6 at JO
We are hosting a Super Sitter class Wednesday, June 6 at the middle school campus. Novant trainers will lead the event.
This six-hour babysitting class teaches students how to provide safe care for young children and explore important safe babysitter strategies. Other topics include the role of the sitter, emergency/safety information, Family and Friends CPR, first aid, understanding children, safety for the sitter and the business of babysitting.  Registration is open to rising 6th-9th graders. The class is from 9 AM- 3:00 PM. Students will need to bring a lunch. 
$55 covers the cost of the class and registration fee. Secure your student's spot by registering below.
