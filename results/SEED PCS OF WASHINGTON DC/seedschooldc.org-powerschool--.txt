The PowerSchool Parent Portal gives parents access to real-time information including attendance, grades and detailed assignment descriptions, and even personal messages from the teacher.
- Access PowerSchool Using this Link -
The official website location is:  
https://seedschool.powerschool.com/public/
Instructions for creating a Powerschool Account:
Click on “Create Account”
Enter Name, email, etc.
Enter a username of your choosing
Enter a password of your choosing - must be 6 characters
Enter the password again
List your children who attend SEED - each child should have an individual Access ID & Access Password
The Access ID is the username we provided
The Access Password is the password we provided
Choose how you’re related to each child
Click on “Enter”
You will then be taken to the Portal’s login page and will sign-in with your newly created username and password
If you have difficulty creating an account or your password needs to be reset, please contact Ms. Corvé Wilcher (202) 248-7773 x5027, Registrar, for assistance.
