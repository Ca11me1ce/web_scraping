Our School
	Located in the Bedford Stuyvesant community of Brooklyn, the school opened in August 2004, and currently serves 720 boys in grades K-8.
	Excellence Boys is housed in a 90,000 square-foot, state-of-the-art facility with a 10,000-volume library, a 500-seat auditorium, music and art studios, a gymnasium, a climbing wall, a rooftop turf field, and sufficient classroom space to house Excellence Boys as a K-8 school.
	We cultivate in our young men the knowledge, skills, and character necessary to succeed academically, embrace responsibility, and become honorable citizens and courageous leaders.
