Resources
Welcome to the Summit High School resource area. This section contains resources you may find useful while attending Summit High School.
In addition, the following school-based resources are available:
SAT/ACT preparation and counseling
Drop-out prevention counseling
Scholarship and financial aid counseling
College and other after-high school transitional services
Personal, crisis, and counseling referral services are available and include:
Independent and social skill training
Parenting education and resources
Arizona Health Care Cost Containment System counseling
Psycho-educational support groups
Drug prevention and intervention services
