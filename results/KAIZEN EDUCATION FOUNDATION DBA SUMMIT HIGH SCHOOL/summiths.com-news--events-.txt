Summer School June 5 - June 16
		5/1/2017				
		0 Comments	
Summit High School - Summer School 2017
Students are able to participate in one class per session. Perfect attendance is required for students to receive .5 credit for a class. All students will be provided a free breakfast and lunch daily.
Classes are a total of ten days, Monday-Friday.  If a student misses more than one hour of any class for any reason, they will not be able to earn credit. No accommodations will be made. Do not sign up for classes if you know you have a prior appointment.
Classes are from 8:00 AM - 2:35 PM (Lunch: 11:30-11:50). The cost is $25. This fee is non-refundable after the first hour of class, on the first day of the class. Any discipline issues will not be tolerated, and students will be removed without a refund.
​Discounted bus tickets for Valley Metro Bus service are available upon request. 
Registration forms
 are available in the office, for more information, please call 602-258-8959 or email 
arnold@summiths.com
.
