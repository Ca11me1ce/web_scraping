From the Desk of the Principal...
Dear Cross Country Elementary Middle SchoolFamilies, Partners and Community,
Itis with great honor and pride that I join the staff as the Principal for the 2015-2016 school year.
 I am very excited to join a school community with dedicated andsupportive families, phenomenal staff, great partners and the brightest andbest children in Baltimore City! The staff, parents and partners have beenoverwhelmingly supportive and welcoming and for that I am grateful. As the yearprogress there will be continuous opportunities to meet and interact with youas we continue our journey to excellence. As the principal I am committed tosupporting all of our students, families, and staff with the transition of anew administrator; and will facilitate a smooth transition while continuing touphold the tradition of raising the bar for all 
students.
