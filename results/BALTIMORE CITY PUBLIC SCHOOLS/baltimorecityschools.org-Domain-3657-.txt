Welcome
Dear Parents, Guardians, andStudents:
Welcomeparents, guardians, and students of Friendship Academy of Engineering andTechnology Middle/High School #339!  Thisyear is destined to be a very exciting one filled with many positivechanges.  We are more determined thanever to partner and work more closely with our parents.  If students are to be successful, we must allwork together.
Thevision of Friendship Academy of Engineering and Technology Middle/High Schoolchanged. Consider your experience here the beginning of your pursuit oflife-long learning and knowledge. All students, faculty, staff, and thecommunity involved have pledged their dedication to ensuring that we create anenvironment of excellence.   Students at FAET are expected to carrythemselves with honor and dignity while faculty and staff are dedicated toproviding the academic rigor.
Understandingthat teaching and learning is the heart of all we do, every student is expectedto come prepared to learn.  The staff ishere to support you through your academic and emotional growth.  Policies and procedures have been developedto create a safe learning environment with the rigor required for meetingacademic success.  With that in mind, 
students can expect to be corrected
.
Thisyear, we plan to continue to work closely with our Board and to secureadditional partnerships.  Our alliancewith the Board should create unique opportunities and experiences forstudents. 
Welook forward to meeting all of you bright and ambitious young men and women. Wehope that you understand and take advantage of the unique opportunitiesavailable to you here at Friendship Academy of Engineering and Technology.  If we all work together, this will be a good and productive year.
 Again,welcome to Friendship Academy of Engineering and Technology Middle/High School.
 With great pride and love,
Tawney Manning, Principal
