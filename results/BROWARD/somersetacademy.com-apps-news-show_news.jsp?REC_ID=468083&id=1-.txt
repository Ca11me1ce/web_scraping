PTSO2018-2019 Officer Elections on May 14, 2018
TheNominations Committee is looking for current PTSO members interested inbecoming members of our 2018-2019 PTSO board. Those current PTSO memberswanting to be nominated for one of our open positions will need to provideinformation for our ballot that will be given out at our May meeting. Anyoneinterested in becoming part of the PTSO board, please e-mail your name, contactinformation, and officer position to 
ptso@somersetacademy.com
to be placed on the nomination list, no later than May 9th. Please indicate inthe e-mail subject: Officer Elections. Voting will take place on the evening ofMay 14th. Nominations to these positions are open.
Whocan serve on the board of the PTSO? Any active PTSO member can be elected toserve. PTSO officers serve a two (2) year term.
PTSOOpen Board Positions: 
President:The president shall preside at all meetings of the PTSO and of the ExecutiveBoard and shall discharge all the executive functions of the PTSO.
VicePresident(s): The Vice President shall act as assistants to the President andshall perform any or all of their duties in the President's absence.
RoomParent Coordinator(s): The Room Parent Coordinator will be responsible fordisseminating advanced notice to all classroom room parents that affect theclass room as well as overall school wide events. 
Treasurer:The treasurer tracks all income and spending, makes financial reports formeetings, and prepares the financial records.
