												LEAP									
The Learning Enrichment After-School Program (L.E.A.P.) was formed by Somerset Academy administration to provide parents of students at its Chapel Trail Campus with before and after care services to students in grades Pre-K - 8th.
L.E.A.P., operated by Somerset administration and staff, provides morning care, commencing at 7:00 a.m., to students of working families who must arrive prior to the commencement of the school day. In addition, L.E.A.P. also provides after care for students of working families until 5:55 p.m. on school days. During the after care hours, L.E.A.P. staff provides homework assistant, recess, arts and crafts, and enrichment activities in language arts and mathematics.
Registration for the L.E.A.P. program is open to Somerset Academy Chapel Trail students in Pre-K through Eighth Grade on a space available basis. In the event that grade-level seats are filled, students will be place on a first come first serve wait list.
