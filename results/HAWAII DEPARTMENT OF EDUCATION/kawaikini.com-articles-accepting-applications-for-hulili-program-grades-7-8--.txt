Accepting applications for Hūlili Program, Grades 7-8!
 Jessell 
 March 13, 2018 
Slideshow
, 
Uncategorized
No Comments
We are now accepting applications for Grades 7-8 for the 2018-2019 school year.
  Students do not need to be fluent in hawaiian language. Interested families should attend an informational session on Monday, March 19, 2018 from 5:00-6:00 pm at the Kawaikini campus, Hale Akamai 1.
  Applications are also available online through the admissions tab.  Application deadline is Friday, April 6, 2018.
  Contact 632-2032 for more information.
