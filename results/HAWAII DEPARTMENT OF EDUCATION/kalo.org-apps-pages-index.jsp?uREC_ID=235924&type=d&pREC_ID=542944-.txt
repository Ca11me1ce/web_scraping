Facility Use Request
If you are interested in holding a meeting, workshop, or an event, we have meeting space available to rent.  Listed below is our current Fee Schedule and instructions on how to request use of our facilities online.  If you would like a printed version, please print the PDF file below.  
Please provide a minimum of 7 days to process your request.
Online
To request a meeting room in our facility, you must first be approved as a group or organization, and then you can go back in to make a room request. 
Please click here FIRST to learn how to log in and submit a request through Community Use
.  Then follow the instructions below: 
Please click on this link to our 
Community Use Website
, where you can see our complete facility calendar and schedule. 
In the upper RIGHTcorner of the page, click on Log in to Request Facility Use.  
Follow the instructions to get set up as an organization.  
If you have questions, please call: 808-890-2508. 
Printed Facility Use Application
Please print the PDF file below, complete it and return via email, fax, or bring it to the KALO offices between the hours of 8:00am - 4:00pm, Monday to Friday.  *Address and fax information is at the bottom of this page.
