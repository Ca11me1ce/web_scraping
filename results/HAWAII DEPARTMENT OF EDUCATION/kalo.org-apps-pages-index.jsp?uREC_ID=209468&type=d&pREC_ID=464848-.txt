Native Hawaiian Library
Hawaiian Language and Cultural Resource Learning Commons
Free Electronic Resources about the People and 'Āina of Hawaii
To our self-directed learners, parents, teachers, and community, this website is a starting point for free electronic resources that are available from many sources, you can access these resources from the library or your home. Click on Links on the right sidebar to access the resources.
If a link has (HSPLS), it means you need to get your user card for the Hawaii State Public Library if you want to use these links. As you discover other resources to share, please let us know.
Studying for the Hawaii Driver's Test?
Check out these FREE practice tests for car, motorcycle and CDL tests.
 You can also view and download the Hawaii Driver's Manual, the Hawaii Motorcycle Guide and the Hawaii CDL Handbook.
