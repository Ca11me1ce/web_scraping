Supporting the Language of Kaua'i, Inc.
SLK
Supporting the Language of Kaua’i, Inc. (“SLK”) is a private, non-profit organization, incorporated and doing business under the laws of the State of Hawaiʻi and tax-exempt under Section 501c3 of the Internal Revenue Code. The mission of Supporting the Language of Kaua’i, Inc. is, “To enhance and preserve the native Hawaiian language.”
For more information on supporting both Kawaikini and Supporting the Languages of Kauaʻi, Inc., please contact Hōkū Kaʻauwai, President and Supporting the Language of Kaua`i at: 
slk.kauai@gmail.com
 or visit the SLK website at 
www.supportingthelanguage.org
