After School Programs
 After School Program
KALO offers many different after school workshops and activities for our keiki attending Kanu o ka ʻĀina NCPCS as well as to the keiki in our community.  Most are free of charge, unless otherwise noted.   
Please see After School Newsletter for current and upcoming program and events.
Our current and upcoming programs are: 
  After School Keiki Care
  Cirque ʻOhana
  Book to Film
  Waikaunu Hula
  Cooking Class
  Not your Mama Science
  Rubiks Club
  Math Club
  Robotics and Drones (Looking for  a mentor/instructor)
Interested?  Please see After School Newsletter for more details.
If you have any ideas or want to create a program, please contact Stasia Nauka at 890-2508 or email stasia@kalo.org.
