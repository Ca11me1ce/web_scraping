Students
Students in grades Kindergarten through 12 have the opportunity to enroll in Kawaikini. Students in grades Kindergarten through 5 are immersed in the Hawaiian language to the fullest extent possible during the day with formal English instruction beginning (in the 5th grade) for one hour per day.
For more info about Kawaikini, call ʻAlohilani Rogers at 632-2032.
