Enrichment
Every year our PTO raises funds so that Van Gogh students can participate in several enrichment opportunities during school hours. Read below to learn more about our Computer Lab and Arts programs. 
Computer Lab
Mr. Gaedeke is here on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays and Fridays to support students in the lab.
Art and Music
Students in grades TK - 5th grade  will receive Vocal Music instruction from Ms. Kim.
Students in TK-2nd will receive a semester of Dance.
Students in 3rd-5th will receive Ballroom Dance sponsored by DANCE WORKS.
