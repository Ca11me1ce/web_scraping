About Us - Tech Center
The Computerwisekids Tech Center is located at
:  
22136 Clarendon St. Woodland Hills. CA 91367
    Phone: 818.206.2628
Our exceptional curriculum and programs combined with skilled instructors allow us to deliver exceptional S.T.E.M. education to students.
Our classes include: coding for beginners thru advance levels in many languages (e.g.Python etc. ), game design, app design (iOS & Android) typing, 3D Printing, robotics and minecraft programming. 
The Tech Center can be reserved for events and parties on Weekends.
