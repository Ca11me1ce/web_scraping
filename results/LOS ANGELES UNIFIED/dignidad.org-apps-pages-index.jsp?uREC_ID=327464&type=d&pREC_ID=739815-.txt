Career Opportunities
Career Opportunities
We are currently seeking highly qualified teachers to teach at Semillas for the 2017-2018 school year. Multiple subject credentialed teacher applicants must be credentialed by the State of California and be fully bilingual in Spanish and English. Single subject teacher applicants must also be fully credentialed in their subject matter - master’s and doctorate degrees are preferred.
Interested applicants for the teacher and educator positions should fax resume to (323) 352-3148.
Or
Apply online:  
Job postings for Semillas Community Schools (EdJoin.org)
Make sure to include the following information:
 1. Position you are applying for
 2. Resume
 3. Cover Letter
 4. Two (2) Letters of Recommendation
 5. Unofficial Transcripts
 6. Copy of Credential(s) (i.e. CBEST, CSET, SSET, RICA, etc.)
Substitute Teacher Positions
Semillas is looking for credentialed, bilingual elementary substitute teachers.
Semillas offers salary and benefits packages comparable to those of the Los Angeles Unified School District. Benefits include medical, dental and vision.
