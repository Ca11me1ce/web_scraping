Weekly Announcements
Good Evening Van Gogh Staff and Families,
          This week is Staff Appreciation Week!!!!!!  Every staff member at Van Gogh contributes to making this an amazing school.  We are so grateful!! Thanks to all of the parents and students working so hard to make this an amazing week!  On Tuesday, May 8th we celebrate our dedicated Teachers!!!  Your hard work and dedication does not go unnoticed!!! We appreciate all that you do!!!  We will begin the week with our Monday Morning Assembly.  Our Valet Volunteers are from Ms. Sowul's class.  Thank you for helping us keep our students safe. We continue with our State testing this week with Ms. Perry, Ms. Malkin-Pica, Ms. Haratian, and Ms. Kazman.  Please make sure your students are well rested and ready to do their best!  We will recognize our TK/Kindergarten/1st grade Students of the Month on Friday, May 11th at 8:01 a.m. in the auditorium. Also on Friday, our 5th graders will be venturing out to Riley's Farm.    Don't forget to purchase our Van Gogh yearbook.  You can purchase it in the office or online at friendsofvangogh.com under Get the yearbook!!  Let's make it a great week of gratitude and kindness!!  I look forward to seeing all of you tomorrow.
Ms. Merloni
