Request for Proposals
We are excited to announce that Camino Nuevo Charter Academy #3 and Camino Nuevo Charter Academy #4 were awarded the After School Education and Safety (ASES) Program to operate after school programs at their respective sites beginning July 1, 2018 through June 30, 2021.
CNCA is seeking a partner organization to operate our after school program at the above sites.  At Camino Nuevo Charter Academy #3, the provider will serve approximately 120 students in grades 5-8 split across two campuses.  At Camino Nuevo Charter Academy #4, the provider will serve approximately 120 students in targeted grades 5-8. Organizations must have experience working with the ASES program to run after school programs, and must have experience working with at-risk youth in Los Angeles. Partnering organizations will employ all program staff and will work with CNCA to develop/enhance the program design and content.
The Request for Proposal is due on Thursday, May 31, 2018.  Interested providers must submit one electronic copy of their response to this Request for Proposal no later than Thursday, May 31, 2018 at 5:00 PM PST.  Submissions must be emailed to 
Erica.Gonzalez@CaminoNuevo.org
. 
If you have any questions regarding the RFP, please email 
Karla.Duarte@CaminoNuevo.org
.  A selection will be made by July 1, 2018.
