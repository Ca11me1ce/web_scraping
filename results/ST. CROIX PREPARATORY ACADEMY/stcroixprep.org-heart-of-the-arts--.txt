Heart of the Arts
Saturday, February 10, 2018
 JX Event Venue
 123 2nd St. N., Stillwater
If you have trouble watching this video, 
please click here
.
Please join us at the Historic JX Event Venue on Saturday, February 10, for a memorable evening supporting Excellence in the Arts at SCPA!
Heart of the Arts is the annual fundraiser for the Fine Arts Department.  The evening is a lively event with savory hors d’oeuvres, delicious sweets, fine wines and beer, live entertainment, raffles, a silent and live auctions and fund-a-need appeal.
Tickets and Auction Items
$35 – Single ticket includes savory hors d’oeuvres and delicious sweets
$45 – after February 3
Purchase tickets and check out the exciting donated items at our 
Event Webpage.
Evening Activities
Bid on an array of unique auction items, play the 50/50 raffle** and wine pull** while enjoying savory hors’ doeuvres  and delicious sweets.
Event Donation Opportunities
We invite you to participate in helping make this year a success.  Please consider contributing an item to the silent or live auction; donate a bottle of wine for the wine pull; or offer a restaurant gift card for $10 or more.
To donate to the auction, please complete the 
Donation Form
.
If donating a bottle of wine or restaurant gift card, no form is needed when dropping off the item at the front office unless you would like a contribution letter/receipt sent to you after the event.
Fund-A-Need Appeal
A special appeal will be made to continue funding for the expansion to the backstage of the Performing Arts Center which will include storage, dressing room, bathroom and set-building space.
