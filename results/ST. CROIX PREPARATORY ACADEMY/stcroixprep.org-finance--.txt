St. Croix Prep Receives 2016 Finance Award
For the 10th year in a row, St. Croix Prep is a recipient of the Minnesota Department of Education’s Finance Award.  The award recognizes schools that exhibit sound fiscal health and financial management policies and procedures.  The criteria for earning a School Finance Award is based on compliance with state statute on a number of financial related issues and other means of demonstrating fiscal health.
