Dallas Can Academy – Ross Ave. Principal’s Message
Welcome to Dallas Can Academy – Ross Ave campus! Home of the Wildcats!
Our faculty and staff are driven by the desire to see our students accomplish their goals. They are committed to communicating with students and parents to provide them every opportunity to succeed. The administration team is dedicated to developing our teachers and staff to provide the best education possible.
We here at Dallas Can Academy – Ross Ave Campus are committed to providing our students with the opportunity to succeed where they might have otherwise failed. As a Ross Ave Family we look forward to every student graduating and preparing them for a world of possibilities.
Principals Bio
Principal Fernando Vadillo
Bachelors in Biochemistry from University of Texas at Dallas
Masters in Biotechnology from University of Texas at Dallas
Masters in Business Administration from University of Dallas
Science Teacher at Dallas Can Academy – Oak Cliff Campus for 3 years before moving on to becoming the Assistant Principal of Curriculum & Instruction at Oak Cliff for an additional 4 years.
He became the Principal of Dallas Can Academy Ross Ave Campus in August 2015.
