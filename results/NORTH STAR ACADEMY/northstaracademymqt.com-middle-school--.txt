Middle School Education
North Star Middle School
North Star Montessori Academy Middle School opened its doors during the 2004-2005 school year and currently houses the 7th and 8th grades. We have unique 7th and 8th grade classrooms. We have a standard classroom and a CEEP program for 7th and 8th grade.
The school is designed to provide:
*A small learning community – PERSONALIZED!
*Mixed age grouping
*Learning that extends into the community
*Project based / hands-on learning
We look forward to working with you this year and welcome you to call or stop by with questions or concerns!
