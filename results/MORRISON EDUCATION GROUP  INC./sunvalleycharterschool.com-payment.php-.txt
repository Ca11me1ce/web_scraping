Make a Payment
Sun Valley Charter School is now accepting online payments for Arizona Tax Credit Contributions, Preschool, Kids Care, breakfasts, lunches, t shirts and Spalding cards via PayPal!
Parents/Guardian please note: There is a $2.50 service fee. For example, if you choose to pay $50.00 only, $47.50 will be applied to your account. In order for the full $50.00 to be applied to your account, you must include the $2.50 service fee so the payment would be $52.50. All payments are made into a family account and are not for an individual child.
        Choose this option to make a payment to Sun Valley for items like t-shirts, lunch, phonogram cards, clubs etc.    
            Choose this option to make a payment for Preschool or the Kids Care Program only.
