Canisius College
Tapestry Charter High School and Canisius College School of Education and Human Services have developed a formal partnership with this mission: “to develop a relationship between Tapestry Charter School and Canisius College that will enhance the child/student educational experience, provide opportunities for growth, and increase the likelihood of success through a sharing of respective resources.”
Expeditionary Learning Schools Outward Bound
Tapestry Charter High School and Expeditionary Learning Outward Bound have established a formal contractual relationship to extend over at least 5 years. Expeditionary Learning Outward Bound staff has been partnered with Tapestry in designing the high school program and in providing on-going consultation and instruction throughout the planning years. This support has included regularly scheduled on-site visits and training by Expeditionary Learning Outward Bound field director and school designers.
