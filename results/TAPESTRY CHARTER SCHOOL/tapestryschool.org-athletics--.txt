Athletics
The Tapestry Charter School mission emphasizes the development of the whole student. Our Athletic Program is one of many components helping to foster a well-rounded individual. Tapestry student-athletes are passionate and take pride in teamwork, dedication and hard work. These traits build character and prepare students for future goals and achievement.
Tapestry is a member of Section VI sports, and is dedicated to quality coaching and student eligibility in the sports offered.
The Tapestry Athlete:
Understands the importance of fitness and its impact on a healthy lifestyle.
Communicates and cooperates with teammates and peers.
Demonstrates competencies in many motor skills and proficiency in few.
Seeks out opportunities in the community for organized sports, exercise, and sports programs.
Sees the connection between good sportsmanship and success in athletics and in real-life social interactions.
Experiences and values the spirit of competition – adversity, accomplishment, coping with loss, dedication, steadfastness.
Is knowledgeable about a variety of sports and can be an active spectator.
Is able to balance academics and sports to become a student-athlete.
Understands the connection between a healthy body and a healthy mind.
Explores college program, careers, and/or employment in athletics.
Applies cognitive knowledge to physical education.
