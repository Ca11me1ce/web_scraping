Illinois Campus
Pre-K 3 to 8th Grade
2602 W. Illinois Ave. Dallas, TX 75233
Phone: 214.333.9330
Office Hours: Monday-Friday 7:30am-4:00pm
Golden Rule School Illinois Campus is ready for the 2017-2018 school year. Our Illinois Campus serves Pre-K4 through 8th grade students. 
Golden Rule Illinois Campus is the first campus of the Golden Rule Charter Schools System. The campus houses the administration section of the school system. The campus offers educational instruction for Pre-kindergarten, kindergarten, elementary, and middle school.
