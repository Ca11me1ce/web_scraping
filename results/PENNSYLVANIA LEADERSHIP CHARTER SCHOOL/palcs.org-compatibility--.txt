Is PALCS Right for You?
Parents and students can answer the following questions to see if cyber school is a good fit for your family. There are no right or wrong answers — the tips you’ll see after you respond are general for every family, and PALCS is a public school available to all students in Pennsylvania — but these questions and responses will help you think about your family participating in cyber school. You might discover an area you want to work on, or you might find you’re already a great fit for cyber school!
