AzMerit Testing Information
		4/16/2018				
		0 Comments	
AzMerit Testing will begin Tuesday, April 10th (AzMerit Writiing). 
AzMerit testing days will follow a Friday Schedule beginning at 8:30 a.m. and ending at 12 p.m. 
Students that took or passed ELA 9-2, 10-2 or 11-2 will be required to take AzMerit testing. 
There will be a continental breakfast offered for students on the days of the test beginning at 7:30 a.m. in room 106. 
Tuesday, April 10th- Writing
Tuesday, April 17th- Reading Pt.1
Wednesday, April 18th- Reading Pt.2
Tuesday, April 24th- Math Pt.1
Wednesday, April 25th- Math Pt. 2
