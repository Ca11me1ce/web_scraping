Special Education
South Pointe High School (SPHS) makes available special education and related services to all students.  It is the philosophy of The Leona Group and SPHS that all children can learn, regardless of disability. Our teachers are trained to teach to diverse learning styles and ability levels. SPHS believes in close collaboration between staff, general education teachers, special education teachers, and parents. This produces the best possible learning outcomes for our exceptional students.
For more information about our special education program, please contact Site SPED Coordinator at 
denise.walker@leonagroup.com
