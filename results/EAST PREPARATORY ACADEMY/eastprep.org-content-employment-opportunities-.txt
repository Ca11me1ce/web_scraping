Employment Opportunities
We are always looking for qualified substitute teachers to assist us when our regular faculty is not in school.  Please stop by our office for an information packet, or call us for more information.
For potential instructional staff at East Preparatory Academy, we believe that our team is what sets us apart from most other schools. We're always looking for dynamic, energetic team members who are ready to help us in creating a world-class school. If you're interested in a challenging and rewarding career at East Preparatory Academy, please send your resume and relevant information to us at info@eastprep.org
