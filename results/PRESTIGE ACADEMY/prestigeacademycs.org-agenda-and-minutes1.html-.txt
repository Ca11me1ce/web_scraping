Board Meeting Agenda and Minutes
​Prestige Academy Board meetings take place every third Tuesday of the month at Prestige Academy Charter School located at 1121 Thatcher Street in Wilmington, DE.  Board meetings are held in the staff conference room on the first floor and begin at 6:00 PM.  Please click on the links below to view Board meeting agendas and minutes.
