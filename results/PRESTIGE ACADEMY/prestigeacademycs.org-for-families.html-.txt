For Families: Choice Application Process
Overview
Prestige Academy will be closing in June 2017. It is extremely important that all of our scholars make a smooth transition into their new schools. All 8th grade families should be completing the process of applying for High School. All 6th and 7th grade families need to make sure that a choice application is completed and submitted by January 11, 2017. You can apply for your school choice at the following website: 
www.schoolchoicede.org
.
Please make sure that you complete his process by January 11, 2017 so that your scholar can get a seat in the school of their choice.
