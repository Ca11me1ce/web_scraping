Useful Links and Resources
Parent Resources
  A list of links for parents: 
  Ohio Department of Education, Testing 
http://www.ode.state.oh.us/proficiency/ 
  Sample proficiency tests and materials can be downloaded for free. Site also includes testing dates, score standards and checklists for graduation. 
  Child Focus 
http://www.child-focus.org 
  Child Focus' mission is to strengthen families and improve quality of life for children. The web site includes information on programs such as therapeutic foster care, child care, education and crisis response. 
  Ohio Headstart 
http://www.ohsai.org 
  The Ohio Head Start Association's web site includes information about all Head Start programs in the state plus links to related sites. 
  Clermont Recovery Center 
http://www.recoveryctr.org 
  Clermont Recovery Center provides alcohol, tobacco and other drug prevention, intervention and treatment. 
Child Care 
http://www.state.oh.us/odjfs/cdc 
  Links to the Ohio Department of Job and Family Services web site, which includes information about finding day care facilities, child care manual, and a child care directory. 
PBS Kids 
http://www.pbskids.org 
  Information about the television station, including programming, games, stories music and pictures to color. 
Zero to Three 
http://www.zerotothree.org 
  Zero to Three is a resource offering information on the first three years of life. Site includes parenting information and tips. 
Child Welfare League of America 
http://www.cwla.org/positiveparenting 
  Tips on parenting, discipline and child development.
