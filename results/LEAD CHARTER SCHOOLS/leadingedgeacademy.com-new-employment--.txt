The Leading Edge Charter Network is a six-campus K-12 charter school with locations in Gilbert, East Mesa, Queen Creek, Maricopa, and Online. We promote an emphasis on advanced academics and character development, utilizing a best practices/data-driven instruction model and current, research-based curriculum. Leading Edge Academy has a history of academic excellence and student achievement. Additionally, we use a values-based program to challenge our students to improve their character and enhance their relationships. These values are trustworthiness, respect, responsibility, fairness, caring, and citizenship. Our schools have small class sizes and a safe family feel.
Spring 2018 Job Fairs
Leading Edge Academy will be hosting two Teacher Job Fairs this spring, one at their Gilbert campus for positions throughout the network and another at their Maricopa location focusing on hiring for that campus specifically.
The Maricopa Campus Job Fair will occur on Thursday, March 1st from 4-7 p.m. and the Leading Edge Network Job Fair will occur at the Gilbert campus on Thursday, March 22nd from 2-6 p.m.
Please schedule an interview appointment by calling the Leading Edge Academy Network office at 480-633-0414 or by emailing

Principal – Gilbert
Leading Edge Academy is accepting applications for the position of Principal at our Gilbert Elementary and Gilbert Early College campuses for the 2018-2019 school year. Must have proven leadership and experience, principal certification, and charter school experience. Current AZ fingerprint clearance card and Bachelor’s degree are required, Master’s degree preferred. Salary is commensurate with experience. Benefits package, including retirement is available, but Leading Edge is not part of the AZ State Retirement System. To apply please download and complete a Teacher/Professional application from the website and email a copy along with your resume and copy of current Arizona fingerprint clearance card to 
resumes@leadingedgeacademy.com.
If you are interested in joining our exceptional professional learning community, please submit the following materials:
