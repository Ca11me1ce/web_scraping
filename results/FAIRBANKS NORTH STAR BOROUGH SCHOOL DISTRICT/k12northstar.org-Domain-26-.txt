Out of Attendance Area Information
Please be aware!
Ben Eielson is currently only registering new students living in our attendance area which includes the Eielson AFB, Salcha, and Moose Creek communities.
We will only be accepting Out of Attendance Area applications for students living in North Pole with proof of a parent being employed on Eielson AFB.
