Online Registration
WELCOME to families that are registering students to attend the Portage Community School District.  The entire registration process is now completed online. This can be done from your home using a computer with internet access, or you can come to our District Registration Office, located in John Muir Elementary School to use a computer.
Before beginning the registration process, parents/guardians should have the following information on hand for each person (parents and students):
Home address and phone numbers.
Parent information, including work and cell phone numbers and email addresses.
Student demographic and health/medication information.
Emergency contact names and phone numbers--two are required.
Proof of residency (picture of document can be uploaded or dropped off)
Please use one of the following links:
New to PCSD Students Registering for 2017-2018 from home or outside the district.
This link takes you to the Online Registration Portal. 
Once you complete this registration form, you will receive an email with further information.
After registering, log in to the Infinite Campus Parent Portal to pay fees and/or make a lunch payment or bring check to school office.
New PCSD Students Registering for 2017-2018 from within the district. (KIOSK Mode)
Current PCSD Students returning to PCSD for 2017-2018
This link takes you to the Infinite Campus Parent Portal.
If you have trouble logging in, contact our Registrar by email or 742-3715.
Click the Online Registration link on the bottom of left side.
The process is quick and easy as you are just updating current information.
Click on Payments to pay online. (Same place as paying for lunch)
If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact Teresa Dyal, District Registrar, at 608-742-3715 or registrar@portage.k12.wi.us.
