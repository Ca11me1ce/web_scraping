Grades 5th-8th
485 Students
24 Teachers
Mission
KIPP Aspire Academy is a free public middle school (grades 5th - 8th). Our mission is to help our students cultivate the academic skills, intellectual habits, and character traits necessary to be successful in the top quality high schools, colleges, and the competitive world beyond. 
Vision
KIPP Aspire Academy provides a unique education to our community of caring, compassionate, critical thinkers with an uncompromising belief in others where all members are accepted, challenged, and pushed to change the world.
