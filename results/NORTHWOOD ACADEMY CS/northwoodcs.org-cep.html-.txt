Community Eligibility Program
​Northwood Academy Charter School will be implementing a new provision known as the Community eligibility Provision (CEP) in the 2016-2017 school year.  This program is available to schools who are participating in the National School Lunch and School Breakfast Programs.  All enrolled student are eligible to receive a nutritious breakfast and lunch at no cost to you.  Free lunch started on the first day of school and breakfast will begin on Tuesday , September 6th.
