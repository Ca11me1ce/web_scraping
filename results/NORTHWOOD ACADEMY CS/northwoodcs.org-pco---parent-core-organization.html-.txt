Our Parent Core Organization
To contact the Parent Core Organization please email them at 
pco@northwoodcs.org
Michael Drummond
Mr. Drummond has been a contributing member to the Northwood community.  He is often seen supporting our sports and other events at school.  Mr. Drummond has been part of the Parent Core Organization for the past 2 years.  Aside from his career, Mr. Drummond spends time with his three children. 
Valarie Jefferson
Mrs. Jefferson has been an essential part of the Northwood community for many years.  She served as President of the Board for 5 years and currently serves as the Board's Vice President.  Mrs. Jefferson has ben part of the Parent Core organization for the past 3 years.  She states, "The goal of the Parent Core Organization is to bring the parent and school community together, it is an organization where all parents are included".  Her work in Early Intervention and with the School District of Philadelphia as part of their Head Start program have provided her insight into the concerns and need for parents involvement in their child's education.  Mrs. Jefferson stays busy with her daughter who is a recent graduate of Northwood and her son who is in 4th grade.  
Tamika Robertson-Joyner
Mrs. Joyner has been a part of Parent Core Organization for the past 2 years.  Both her career and private time reflect her passion for helping others.  Mrs. Joyner is a Training and Development Instructor with the City of Philadelphia.  In her spare time she also volunteers with the Girl Scouts and the Youth Ministry at Bethany Baptist Church.  She feels that the Parent Core Organizations's goal is to bridge the gap between home and school by creating an environment where parents feel connected to the Northwood community through volunteering and social events.  all of the experiences offered by the Parent Core Organization are to "enhance the future for the students".  Mrs. Joyner stays busy with her three children.  Her favorite quote is, "Why fit in , when you were born to stand out?" - Dr. Suess
Katrina Maddox
Mrs. Maddox has invested 3 years of her time into the Parent Core Organization at Northwood.  Through her dedication to the Parent Core Organization she has been able to allow our students to experience opportunities such as the Book Fair and Holiday Shop, along with countless other activities.  Prior to committing to the Parent Core Organization, Mrs. Maddox worked at the Pennsylvania School for the Deaf and at Lawncrest Preschool.  Most of Mrs. Maddox's free time is spent with her two children.  Mrs. Maddox believes that the Carl Gustav Jung quote best describes the importance of the Parent Core Organization - "You are what you do, not what you say you do".
