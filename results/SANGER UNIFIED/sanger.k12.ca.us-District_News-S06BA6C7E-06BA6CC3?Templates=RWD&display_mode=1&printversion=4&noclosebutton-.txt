Congratulations to Leslie Hoffman
Friday, April 20, 2018
Congratulations to Leslie Hoffman on her selection as the recipient of the 2018 Carolyn Dobbs Special Education Teacher of the Year Award. This award is presented by the Kremen School of Education and Human Development at Fresno State to recognize the importance of the work of special education professionals. Ms. Hoffman is a Resource Specialist Program (RSP) Teacher at Reagan Elementary and is devoted to her students. Thank you Leslie for all that you do for the students of Sanger Unified!
