Kindergarten Registration
Kindergarten/TK Online Registration available March 1st
ONLINE REGISTRATION IS EASY!!! (Online registration BEGINS March 1st – “Walk in” registrations still accepted during the week of March 19th).
 Click here to start
Input all information required
Print receipt of completion
Present documents for verification at school site on designated day during week of March 
19th.
If you need internet access, please contact your school site for information on computer availability.
 información en español
To be eligible to register for 
Kindergarten, students must be 5 years old on or before September 1, 2018. 
To be eligible to register for 
Transitional Kindergarten, students must be 5 years old between September 2-December 2, 2018.
Upon completion of online registration, the parent or legal guardian will need to visit their school site to provide documentation for verification and completion of the registration process. You must have a valid address within the boundaries of Sanger Unified to register. Documents needed for verification include:
Proof of residency (utility bill, etc.)
Birth certificate, baptismal records, or statement of attending physician must be presented to verify age of child.
Completed physical exam dated after March 1, 2017. An appointment for an exam will NOT be accepted.
Written evidence of up-to-date immunizations for: Polio, MMR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella), DTP (Diphtheria-Tetanus-Pertussis), Hepatitis B (3 shots over a 6-month period) and Varicella (chickenpox) for more detailed information 
click here
.
Documentation of either TB Testing or TB Risk Assessment completed within 18 months prior to initial entry to Sanger Unified School District.
Oral health assessment within the last 12 months (submit prior to K completion).
AB 277 Alert: Personal belief immunization waivers are
 no longer accepted.
