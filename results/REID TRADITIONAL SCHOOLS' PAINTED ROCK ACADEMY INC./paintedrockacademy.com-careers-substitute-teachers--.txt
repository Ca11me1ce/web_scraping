SUBSTITUTE TEACHERS
We always have a need for substitute teachers and welcome applications all year round.
Substitutes may choose the days and grade levels they would like to teach. Substitutes work on an on-call basis. Painted Rock Academy compensates substitutes with a teachers’ certification $100 per day. Substitutes without a certification are compensated at $85 per day. Substitutes must produce a valid Arizona state fingerprint card, which school personnel can help in obtaining.
Position Available:
SUBSTITUTE TEACHER
For the current school year. Grades K – 8
Employment Status:
On call, depending on availability
Job Description:
Provides classroom instruction as directed by teacher lesson plans and grade papers according to teacher’s directions.
Minimum Requirements:
Bachelor’s Degree and ability to pass background check are required.
Desired Qualities:
Previous teaching experience is desired.
Salary Range:
Certified: $100 per day | Non-certified: $85.00 per day
Long-term assignments: above pay plus $20 per day.
(applies after the twentieth consecutive day of assignment)
Closing Date:
Applications always accepted.
