PRIVACY STATEMENT
Information Collected on Our Website
Our web server automatically recognizes every visitor’s IP address, but not their e-mail address or name. We collect aggregate information on the web pages that are visited. This data is stored in the web logs residing on our server. We collect e-mail addresses and names of those who communicate with us via e-mail or contact form, along with any information volunteered by the visitor. Visitors who do not wish their e-mail and other information to be saved by the school may prevent the information from being saved by submitting a request via e-mail, fax or telephone by calling the Reid Traditional Schools office at 623-478-2344.
Any information collected is not shared with any other organization (unless specifically required by state and local authorities) and is not released or used by anyone other than Painted Rock Academy authorized individuals.
Ad Servers
We have no relationship with any ad server company.
Security
We have appropriate security measures in place in our physical facilities to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from web users.
In the future, should we decide to use visitor information in new ways not previously disclosed in our privacy policy or if our information practices change, we will post the policy changes to our website and notify patrons of the change.
