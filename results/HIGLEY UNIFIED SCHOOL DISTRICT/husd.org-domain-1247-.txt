I'm Mrs. Holloway. I look forward to year 5 at Cooley Middle School! I have previously taught 7th Grade Language Arts for 8 years at Gateway Pointe. Before that, I taught 9th grade English in a little city named Compton, California. I have loved all of my teaching experiences and I hope to make this another great year! I love reading and writing, I have a little obsession with Captain Jack Sparrow and Wolverine, and I have always wanted to be a teacher! Some of my favorite books are 
Harry Potter
, 
The Hunger Games
, and most young adult fiction!  
I am originally from Southern California and I try to visit whenever I can. I lived in Utah for a few years while I attended college at Utah Valley State College (now UVU). When I finished college, I got married and moved back to California where I had my first 2 years of teaching. I really loved it, but my family was ready for a change of location so we moved to Arizona. I have enjoyed living here, but I'm still getting used to the heat!
I hope that we can have a great year learning and growing together.  Please let me know if I can help you with anything or answer any of your questions. Go COUGARS!!!
Email: 
elizabeth.holloway@husd.org

MA, Ed: Curriculum & Instruction
