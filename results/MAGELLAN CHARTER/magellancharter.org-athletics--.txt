Athletics
Teaching Leadership, Sportsmanship, and Teamwork.
Middle School Athletics:
The Magellan Charter School competes in interscholastic middle school athletics. Magellan is a member of the Capital Area Middle School Conference. Student athletes must meet vigorous academic and attendance requirements in order to compete and represent Magellan.
Physical:
All Magellan student athletes must have a current physical in order to try out for a team. Physicals are good for one calendar year. Physicals must be submitted before the  first day of tryouts.
