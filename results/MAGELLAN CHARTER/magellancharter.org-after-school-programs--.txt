After-School Programs and Activities
The Magellan Charter School offers an after-school program for our students. Independent vendors conduct these programs/activities, which are learning opportunities with quality supervision conducted in a fun and social atmosphere. There is an additional fee payable to the course instructor for these programs.
.Independent teachers offer the following programs, and payment is made directly to the sponsor.
Please see contact address for registration, cost, and more information.
Course offerings may change yearly based on availability of instructor and enrollment.
Classes fill quickly, so please turn in your registration as soon as possible.
