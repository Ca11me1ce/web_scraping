2018-2019 PreK-6th Student Admission Applications
Dear Prospective Minneola Charter Schools Parent/Guardian:
We are excited you have chosen Minneola Charter School as the school choice to educate your child/children. MCS is a tuition-free public school that serves PreK-6th Grade. Although the application period has passed, families may continue to submit an enrollment application for available student seats. Upon submission of this application, the registrar will review your application to ensure eligibility requirements are met. Families will receive an email regarding enrollment status within 2 business days of application submission. The enrollment status includes one of the following options: 1) Eligible to enroll, seats available. 2) Eligible to enroll, wait listed for available seat to open. 3) Not eligible to enroll, (reason will be stated in the body of the email).
MCS accepts applications and enrolls students until all available seats have been filled. Wait lists are maintained if applicants exceed available seats. Wait lists do not carry over from year to year. For Enrollment Eligibility and Admission information please view the "Information" tab on the MCS webpage www.lake.k12.fl.us/moe.
We welcome the opportunity to educate your child/children and hope your child is selected for enrollment and can grow with us as we "Gallop to Success."
Please fill out a separate application for each child.
