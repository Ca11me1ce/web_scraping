NEWS FEATURES:
Santa Day!
Ten days before traveling the world with his reindeer and sleigh, Santa Claus paid a special visit to Lake Hills School, our county’s Exceptional Student Education center. Accompanying Santa were groups bringing Christmas cheer: PAWS therapy dogs and their owners, RealLife Christian Academy’s student choir, and This Boat Rocks! Dragonboat team of the Villages, who donated a wrapped Christmas gift for every student at Lake Hills. Volunteers were Irene McGee, Linda Weelock (Clown Ally #179) and Mike Hilliard. The school staff would like to thank everyone involved in making it an amazing day!
