Welcome
Welcome to Eustis Middle School, Home of the Mustangs!
EMS Mission:
  To foster a safe, positive and engaging learning environment for our students, which promotes the value of an education.
EMS Vision:
  Eustis Middle School is the home of a faculty, staff and student body who believe in each other’s willingness to grow and adapt, in order to meet the challenges faced by today’s students.
