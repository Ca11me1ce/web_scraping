Smith Academy for Excellence		
Enrollment Policies
Pre-Enroll Online Now!
Smith Academy for Excellence operates as a single gender boys’ school
under P.L. 1-2005, SEC.8, amended by P.L.91-2011,  SEC14. The school is
open to any male student who resides in Indiana per IC § 20-24-5. 
Smith Academy for Excellence admits students of any race, color, national
and ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities
generally accorded or made available to students at the school. It does not
discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin, religion
or ancestry or disability in administration of its educational policies, admissions policies,
and athletic and other school-administered programs. 
As a charter school, SAFE is pleased to offer a high quality education 
tuition free!
