Leona Group Support Services
In the Midwest, The Leona Group provides a comprehensive package of management services to its client boards of directors.
The Leona Group and its team members operate under the driving philosophy that all students, when provided the appropriate tools and support, can achieve at high levels. Whole-child growth and development is at the forefront. 
Academic success, fiscal responsibility and compliance with federal, state and local regulations are steadfast objectives of our business model. The Leona Group provides a full complement of education management services including expertise and delivery of educational, business and operational services, including:
Academic support
Budget and finance
Communications & marketing
Human resources
Legal & risk management
Technology
Special projects
School Start-Up
The Leona Group gets schools up and running, from the initial charter  application, grant applications, building and site acquisition, to  development of the academy's organizational structure. We are there,  each step of the way, to assist fledgling schools and to ensure  long-term success. 
Facility
 The Leona Group assists in identifying the best possibilities for a  school location. Our experienced team offers advice and provides direct  assistance to negotiate leases and purchases.  
Financial
 Professional support is provided in  arranging for short-term loans,  debt restructuring, grant-writing and other financial needs. The Leona  Group has earned a solid reputation for its sound financial management.
