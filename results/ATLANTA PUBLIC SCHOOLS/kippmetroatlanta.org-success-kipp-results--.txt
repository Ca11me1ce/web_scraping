KIPP Results
Success at KIPP Metro Atlanta Schools
KIPP Metro Atlanta students are proving that great teaching, college-preparatory academics, focus on character, and safe and nurturing school environments can help all students reach their full potential. Our students’ results continue to prove what is possible.
School Performance
KIPP schools outperform their district peers on The College and Career Readiness Performance Index (CCRPI), Georgia’s comprehensive school performance and improvement metric. The following are CCRPI Results from the 2016-17 school year.
KIPP STRIVE, WAYS, and Vision Academies
KIPP’s three middle schools in Atlanta Public Schools each scored above the district average.
Note: Although KIPP middle schools start in 5th grade, scores reflect 6th-8th grade in alignment with district middle schools in order to provide an apples-to-apples comparison
KIPP South Fulton Academy
KIPP South Fulton Academy scored first among middle schools in South Fulton, with a CCRPI score 12 points higher than the average for the entire county.
KIPP Atlanta Collegiate
KIPP Atlanta Collegiate scored above the Atlanta Public Schools’ average for all high schools in the district.
KIPP STRIVE Primary School
KIPP STRIVE Primary School is the first KIPP Metro Atlanta elementary school to receive a CCRPI score since it was the first to serve all grade levels K-4. All KIPP Metro Atlanta elementary school will receive CCRPI scores for the 2017-18 school year.

KIPP METRO ATLANTA
KIPP Metro Atlanta Schools is part of the growing network of KIPP (Knowledge Is Power Program) public charter schools across the nation. KIPP is a network of free, public charter schools open to all students that prepare students in underserved communities for success in college and choice-filled lives.
