Westchester Drama Club
Welcome to the
Westchester Drama Club!!
Any Westchester student who is interested in theatre may join! Listen for our first meeting time on the morning announcements or come by and look on the Call Board outside the theatre (across from the Choir Room) or this page for information!
Any techies out there? Come by the Call Board and sign up to help on a crew (or 2) and help out with our fall musical
INTO THE WOODS! See Ms. Kreisner if you have any questions!!
