Join the DRIHSCS Family!
Community charter school seeks Mathematics Teachers (multiple openings) for grades 6-12. Provide mathematics instruction. Create lesson plans, administer tests and assess curricular needs. Communicate recommendations to students, parents, and colleagues. Requires: Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics, Education, or related; at least 1 semester (5 months) of experience as a math teacher or student/practicum teacher; ability to teach content from 6th grade math to AP Calculus, Statistics and AP Statistics; plus passing score on a Mathematics Teacher Certification Exam, such as NYS Mathematics CST or Praxis II: Mathematics. Forward resumes to: Dr. Richard Izquierdo Health & Science Charter School, 800 Home Street, Bronx, NY 10456  
Attn: Rose Tomasi

ABOUT HEALTH & SCIENCE SCHOOL: 
We are a 6 -12th-grade community charter school now in its 7th year. This school year we are fully enrolled with the addition of our first graduating 12th-grade class. We are actively developing a middle school dual-language program that will begin in September of 2017.
OUR MISSION: Dr. Richard Izquierdo Health & Science Charter School provides our Scholars with a nurturing and challenging educational experience to develop their abilities and address the health and economic disparities in our community. Upon graduation, our Scholars will be prepared for the highest levels of college achievement, gainful employment as certified health care professionals, and a commitment to serve others as they pursue rewarding lives and respected careers for themselves.
OUR OPERATING VALUES:
Everything with integrity
Thoughtful and intentional
Clear communication
Leading with urgency
Relentless optimism
Professionalism
