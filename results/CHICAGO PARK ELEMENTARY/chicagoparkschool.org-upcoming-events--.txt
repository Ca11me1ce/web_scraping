Harvest Festival
Friday October 7, 4pm-7pm
Chicago Park PTA Harvest Festival is Friday October 7, 4pm-7pm.  Join us at this fair-style event. We’ll have fun booths, treats, and a delicious BBQ. 50% of the booths profit goes to the classrooms.  Tickets are 25¢ a piece.  
Donation of candy and/or small prizes welcome.  Drop at school’s office on festival’s week.
Your teacher or classroom liaison will be in touch with you soon about helping for the classroom booth. Your participation will MAKE this event. All hands on deck please and the kids will have a BLAST!
