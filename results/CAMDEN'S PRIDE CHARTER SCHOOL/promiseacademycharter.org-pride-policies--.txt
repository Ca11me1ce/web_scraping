Student Code of Conduct
At the end of each marking period, the student’s attendance and academic requirements will be evaluated and determinations will be made as to whether promotion is in jeopardy. Parent/Teacher conferences will be held at the end of each marking period (1
st
, 2
nd
 and 3
rd
) to discuss report cards and progress. Report cards must be picked up by the next field trip, the student will not be allowed to attend the field trip until the report card is picked up.
ATTENDANCE REQUIREMENT
The student must have an attendance for the year with no more than 10% unexcused absences. ( 18 days unexcused)
ACADEMIC REQUIREMENT
The student must demonstrate progress in all major subjects.
STANDARDIZED TEST PARTICIPATION
The students are required to participate in all standardized test administered during the school year. The result will be reviewed to decide promotion.
STUDENT PORTFOLIO
The students are required to complete a number of assignments and projects that will be compiled in a portfolio.
LIGHT’S RETENTION SCALE
For those students who are in jeopardy of promotion, the LRS will be completed to assist in determining promotion.
