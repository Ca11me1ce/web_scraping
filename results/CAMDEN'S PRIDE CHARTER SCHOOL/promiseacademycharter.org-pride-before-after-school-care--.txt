Before & After School Care
Before School  (7:30-8:30)
Students may arrive at school starting at 7:30. Parents who drop-off before 7:20 must remain with their child until 7:20 a.m. Transportation is not provided for the students for Before School. Students must have turned in a permission slip to attend Before School. Breakfast is served for students from 7:30 to 8:25 a.m.
Regular School Day (8:30-3:00)
The hours of school are from 8:30 – 3:00. Students are dismissed at 3:00. Transportation is provided for those students who qualify for busing. A parent and/or guardian must be at the bus stop to pick up the child. A parent and/or guardian must pick up non-bus riders. Students are not allowed to walk home.  Middle School students are not allowed to pick-up students.
Half School Day (8:30-12:30)
The hours of a Half Day are from 8:30-12:30. There is no After School Program on half days. There is Before School. There is bus transportation for those that qualify. 
After School (3:00-4:00)
The After School program provides additional academic programming and enrichment for the students.   Parents must have filled out a permission slip in order to stay for the program. Transportation is not provided for students staying in the after-school program.  A parent and/or guardian must pick up non-bus riders. Middle School students are not allowed to pick up students.  If a student is picked-up late 3 times, the student is no longer allowed to attend the after school program. “Late” is defined as after 4:10.
After “After” School  (4:00-5:00) for 3rd and 4th Grade
The After “After” School program is for 3rd and 4th Grade.  It is a NJASK skill builder and focuses on test preparation for the NJASK.  An Additional permission slip is needed and the program does not begin until after the 2nd Marking Period.
