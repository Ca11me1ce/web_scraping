Evolution Academy Beaumont and Houston Hurricane Harvey Relief Fund
Hurricane Harvey has caused catastrophic flooding and damage in Houston, Beaumont, Port Arthur, Texas and the surrounding areas. Many are without basic needs and services. Evolution Academy Houston and Beaumont campuses, students, staff, faculty, and community are included in this natural disaster.
The Evolution Academy Hurricane Harvey Relief Fund will provide relief to our students their families who have been affected by this tragedy in the form of emergency supplies like food, water, clothes, and other basic needs.
