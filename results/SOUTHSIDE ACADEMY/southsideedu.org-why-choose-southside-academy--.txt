About Warrior Time
<img src="https://static1.squarespace.com/static/5800d74a44024355541dd41f/t/590c8df1725e256653138e4c/1493994998814/"  />
Warrior Time is the afterschool program at South Side Academy.  During Warrior Time, students are offered assistance with homework, provided dinner, and participate in numerous academically and physically based programs including:
Gardening
Readers Theater
Computer Classes
Newspaper
Challenge 24 math
Team building
Scientific Method Activities
Power Hour Skill Building
