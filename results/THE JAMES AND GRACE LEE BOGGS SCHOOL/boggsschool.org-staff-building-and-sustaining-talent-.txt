Staff: Building and Sustaining Talent
Apply for a position at the Boggs School!  We happily accept resumes all year. Please include a cover letter with your application, which can be submitted as described below.
14524967_10153799791672274_5648973958179742089_o.jpg
14567340_10153799791957274_3269993917515447724_o.jpg
The Boggs School is a K-8 community-based public charter elementary school on Detroit’s east side.  We follow a place-based educational model and our mission is to nurture creative, critical thinkers who contribute to the well-being of their communities.  If you yourself are a creative, critical thinker who wants to contribute to the well-being of a school community as well as the Detroit community and you are able to thrive in a collaborative environment, please apply! CERTIFIED TEACHERS ONLY, PLEASE.
PLEASE SUBMIT  A CURRENT RESUME AND A COVER LETTER BY MAIL TO: Julia Putnam, 4141 Mitchell St., Detroit, MI 48207 OR BY EMAIL TO: julia.boggsschool@gmail.com.
Teachers of arts, world languages, P.E., music, and other specialty areas should send a resume and a letter of interest to Marisol Teachworth at marisol.boggsschool@gmail.com. Certification may not be required.
